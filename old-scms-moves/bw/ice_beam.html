Description

Deals damage and has a 10% chance to freeze the target.

Competitive Use

The second half of the acclaimed BoltBeam combination, Ice Beam is powerful and perfectly accurate. It is freely available as a Technical Machine, thus allowing many Pokemon, most notably Water-types, to learn it. Related Moves

Blizzard is a more powerful but less accurate version of Ice Beam, with fewer PP. It has a useful niche on hail teams, where it has 100% accuracy.

Hidden Power Ice is a weaker alternative that's typically used when Ice Beam is not available.
