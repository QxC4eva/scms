[SHORT DESC] Hits 2-5 times in one turn. [LONG DESC]

Description

Hits 2-5 times in one turn. There is a 37.5% chance to hit either 2 or 3 times, and a 12.5% chance to hit either 4 or 5 times. If one hit faints a Substitute, the move will be halted.

Competitive Use

Fury Attack should not be used competitively. Its variable Base Power and imperfect accuracy make it too unreliable, especially when stronger options such as Body Slam and Double-Edge exist.

Related Moves

Body Slam is a more powerful alternative which boasts a 30% paralyze chance.

Double-Edge is a more powerful alternative, but there is recoil damage.
