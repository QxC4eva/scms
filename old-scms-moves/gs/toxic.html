[SHORT DESC] Inflicts intensifying poison on the target. [LONG DESC]

Description

Inflicts intensifying poison on the target that does 1/16 max HP damage the first turn, 2/16 max HP on the second turn, then 3/16, etc., adding 1/16 to the damage each turn. It maxes out at 15/16 max HP damage. The intensifying factor is reset when the inflicted Pokemon switches out.
