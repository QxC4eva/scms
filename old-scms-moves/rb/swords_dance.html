[SHORT DESC] Boosts Attack 2 stages. [LONG DESC]

Description

Boosts the user's Attack by two stages.

Competitive Use

Swords Dance is one of the best stat-boosting moves in the game. Any physical attacker will enjoy the +2 Attack boost Swords Dance has to offer. Venusaur uses it to great extent, 2HKOing Chansey with Body Slam after the boost.
