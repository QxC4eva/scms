Overview
########
- Good Eviolite-boosted bulk
- Hits 19 speed, only a handful of unboosted mons are able to outspeed
- Recovery in Morning Sun
- WoW and Flame Body to deter physical attackers
- Can act as a sun setter, benefits from the weather itself, thanks to Morning Sun and boosted STAB
- Relies on recoil moves for offense
- Weak to SR

Moves
========

- Flare Blitz hits very hard thanks to 17 Attack
- Morning Sun heals 50% health, negating FB recoil and SR damage
- Will-O-Wisp cripples switch-ins like Gligar
- Sunny Day boosts Morning Sun recovery and Flare Blitz damage
- Wild Charge gives Ponyta super-effective coverage on water-types and Archen

Set Details
========

- Max speed to speed tie 19 speed mons (Gligar, Murkrow, Modest Yanma)
- 156 Atk EVs hits 17 attack (will do specific calcs later) , the rest is thrown into bulk to hit 21/19/21 defenses with Eviolite
- Eviolite gives Ponyta very good bulk from both sides
