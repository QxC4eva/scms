Thanks to the wonders of OpenSearch, our Smogon search engine is available as a browser plugin for Firefox 2+ and Internet Explorer 7+.

Browsers (pictured: Firefox 2) that support plugin auto-discovery can easily add Smogon search to their browser by opening their plugin list and selecting "Add Smogon".

An installed search plugin. Happy searching! For tips and tricks, try the help page.
