The new release is upon us and, man, has it been a long time coming!

First and foremost, our long-time editor, Setsuna, has retired. Setsuna was best known for his leading role in The Bourne Trilogy, but he was also our team leader for roughly the past two and a half years. We wish him all the best in his future endeavors, whatever they may be.

The Smog goes on, though! And, since I "acquired" the password to the PayPal account that Setsuna inherited from Rising_Dusk oh-so-many issues ago, I will be taking his place as The Smog's new corrupt overlord editor.

As always, a huge thanks goes out to all the contributors. I’d especially like to note that the stunning artwork you see on the cover of this issue is courtesy of Magistrum. Be sure to let us know what you think in the new-and-improved reader feedback survey, as well. Thanks for reading!

—Toast++
