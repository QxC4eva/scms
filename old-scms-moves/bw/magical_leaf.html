[SHORT DESC] Ignores evasion modifiers. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage and ignores all accuracy and evasion modifiers.

Competitive Use

Magical Leaf has little competitive use. Its Base Power is too low, and it is outclassed by stronger moves such as Giga Drain and Energy Ball. However, it can be paired with Technician for a powerful never-miss move.

Related Moves

Giga Drain is a stronger alternative that heals the user's HP.

Energy Ball is a more powerful alternative that has a chance to lower the opponent's Special Defense stat by one stage.

Leaf Storm is a much more powerful alternative, but it lowers the user's Special Attack stat by two stages when it is used.

Aerial Ace is a Flying-type equivalent.

Faint Attack is a Dark-type equivalent.

Magnet Bomb is a Steel-type equivalent.

Shadow Punch is a Ghost-type equivalent.

Shock Wave is an Electric-type equivalent.

Swift is a Normal-type equivalent.
