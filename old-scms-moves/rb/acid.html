[SHORT DESC] 10% chance to lower Def 1 stage. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage and has a 10% chance to lower target's Defense one stage.

Competitive Use

Acid is, like most low-power moves, completely useless competitively. If you want to lower the opponent's Defense, Screech is easily better. For a more powerful Poison-type move, Sludge is much more powerful. Don't bother using it, ever.

Related Moves

Leer deals no damage but lowers Defense by one stage 100% of the time.

Screech deals no damage but lowers Defense by two stages 100% of the time.
