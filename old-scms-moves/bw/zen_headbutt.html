[SHORT DESC] 20% chance to flinch the target. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage and has a 20% chance to flinch the target.

Competitive Use

Zen Headbutt is usually the "Psychic-type STAB of choice" for physically-oriented Psychic-types, facing little competition from Psycho Cut thanks to its higher Base Power and much better distribution. The flinch chance is a nice bonus, but shouldn't be counted on, unless you are running Jirachi.

Related Moves

Psycho Cut is a less powerful but more accurate option with a high critical hit rate.
