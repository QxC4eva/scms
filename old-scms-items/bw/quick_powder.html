[SHORT DESC] Doubles Ditto's Speed. [LONG DESC]

Description

Doubles Ditto's Speed stat. If Ditto transforms into another Pokemon, the boosts are lost.

Competitive Use

At first, the ability to Transform into the foe first is vital for Ditto, as it cannot withstand even the slightest of attacks thanks to its miserable defenses. However, because this boost is lost once Ditto transforms, Quick Powder is rather useless, even if you happen to be using Ditto in the first place.
