[SHORT DESC] A Ball used to catch Pokemon that is more effective against Pokemon that evolve with a Moon Stone. [LONG DESC]

Description

A type of Ball used to catch Pokemon. Cannot be used during Trainer, Link, or Wi-Fi Battles.

Moon Ball has a capture modifier of *1. When used against a Pokemon in the Nidoran, Clefairy, Jigglypuff, or Skitty family, this modifier becomes *4.
