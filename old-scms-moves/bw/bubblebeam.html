[SHORT DESC] 10% chance to lower target's Speed by 1 stage. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage and has a 10% chance to lower the target's Speed by one stage.

Competitive Use

BubbleBeam should generally not be used competitively, as its Base Power is much lower than those of Surf and Hydro Pump, and its secondary effect is neither likely to occur nor especially useful when it does.

Related Moves

Surf is a more powerful and reliable alternative with no added effect.

Scald is a more powerful and reliable alternative that may inflict a burn on the opponent.

Hydro Pump is a far more powerful alternative with lower accuracy.
