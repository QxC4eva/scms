[SHORT DESC] Hits twice in 1 turn. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage and always hits twice unless the target is KOed by the first hit or a Substitute is broken by the first hit.

Competitive Use

This move is too weak to be used at all.

Related Moves

Hi Jump Kick is a much stronger Fighting-type alternative for Hitmonlee that will inflict 1 HP recoil should the user miss.

Submission is a stronger Fighting-type alternative that inflicts recoil on the user.

Bonemerang is a more powerful Ground-type variant with 90% accuracy.
