[SHORT DESC] Enemies' contact moves can paralyze them. [LONG DESC]

When an enemy uses a contact move against this Pokémon, there is a 30% chance of paralyzing the enemy. If the attack does no damage, Static does not activate. Static still has a chance to paralyze Ground-type Pokémon, and those with the Volt Absorb and Motor Drive abilities.

When a Pokémon with this ability is first in the party, wild encounters with Electric Pokémon have a higher chance of occurring.
