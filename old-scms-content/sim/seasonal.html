[title] Pokemon Showdown Seasonal Ladder [head] [page]
By Joim.

Seasonal Ladder is a tier hosted on Pokemon Showdown. Much like Random Battle, the Seasonal Ladder lets you play with randomly generated teams; however, not every Pokemon can be randomly generated. The Pokemon that may be generated are based on themes relating to the time of the year. The current theme is Fabulous February.

This month's theme is chinese new year and valentine's. The Pokémon that may be found on this Seasonal are mainly red, horses and otherwise simply chinese based. The game type is Doubles since they are all couples.
