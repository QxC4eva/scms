Introduction

Hordes are a new type of battle introduced in Pokémon X and Y. These battles force the trainer to confront five wild Pokémon at a time instead of the usual one. Often, horde battles will contain five of the same Pokémon; however, there are certain areas which feature horde battles containing multiple species. While these battles can prove challenging during the main story, they serve as a quick means of EV training Pokémon in the post-game.

Horde Tips

  • Use Sweet Scent or Honey to initiate a horde battle while standing in tall grass.
  • Sweet Scent and Honey will fail if used on a route in which it is raining.
  • Use a powerful spread move like Petal Blizzard or Surf to KO all five Pokémon at the same time!
  • You gain EVs and experience for all five horde Pokémon defeated in battle.

EV Training Tips

  • A Pokémon gains EVs when it gains experience.
  • To wipe a Pokémon's EVs, use a Reset Bag from Super Training.
  • Pokérus doubles the number of EVs your Pokémon gains in battle.
  • Macho Brace doubles the number of EVs your Pokémon gains in battle.
  • Power Items (Brace, Anklet, etc.) grant an additional 4 EVs in a specific stat per Pokémon defeated.
  • With Exp. Share ON, the entire party gains EVs.

Horde EV Math

You can calculate how many EVs you will gain from a horde battle by using the following formula:

EVs = (Total_Horde_EVs + 20 A)*B*C
  • Total_Horde_EVs = EVs found on the far right of the tables below
  • A = Power Item Stat (Weight = HP, Bracer = Atk, Belt = Def, Lens = SpA, Band = SpD, Anklet = Spe)
  • B = Pokérus (Not infected = 1, Infected = 2)
  • C = Macho Brace (Not equipped = 1, Equipped = 2)

Example: A Pokémon with Pokérus defeats a Gulpin horde while holding a Power Weight.

EVs = (5 HP + 20 HP)*2*1
EVs = (25 HP)*2
EVs = 50 HP
