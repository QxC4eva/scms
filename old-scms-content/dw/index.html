The BW generation began in 2010 following the Japanese release of Pokémon Black and White. It introduced 156 new Pokémon, triple and rotation battles, and the Pokémon Dream World. This section is focused on the competitive scene as if Dream World were the real world.

BW and Dream World is played online on the simulator Pokémon Online.
