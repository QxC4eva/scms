[title] Create-A-Pokémon Process Guide [head] [page]

The Create-A-Pokémon project (CAP) creates new Pokémon in a step-by-step process, led by a Topic Leadership Team (TLT) with each step representing one aspect of a new Pokémon. Each part of the Pokémon is discussed and voted on by the community. Details of the process can be found below.

  • Events
    • Policy Review Committee Applications
    • Topic Leader and Topic Leadership Team Nominations
    • Topic Leader and Topic Leadership Team Selection
    • Concept Submissions
    • Concept Assessment
    • Threats Discussion
    • Stat Limits Discussion
    • Stat Spread Submissions
    • Art Submissions
    • Counters Discussion
    • Move Discussion
    • Movepool Submissions
    • Sprite Submissions
    • Final Product
    • Playtesting
    • Analysis
  • Process Rules
  • Topic Leader
  • Policy Committee
