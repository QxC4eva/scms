[SHORT DESC] Summons permanent sandstorm. [LONG DESC]

When this Pokémon is brought out, a permanent sandstorm covers the battlefield. It can only be removed by bringing out a Pokémon with Drizzle, Drought, or Snow Warning, or by using Rain Dance, Sunny Day, or Hail.

If two Pokemon with weather-changing abilities are sent out at the same time, the weather of the Pokemon with the lower Speed stat will prevail.

Related Abilities

Drizzle is a Rain equivalent.
Drought is a Sun equivalent.
