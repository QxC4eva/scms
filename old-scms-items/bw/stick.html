[SHORT DESC] Raises Farfetch'd's critical hit rate two stages. [LONG DESC]

Description

If held by Farfetch'd, this item raises the critical hit ratio of the holder by two stages. No effect if held by any other Pokemon.

Competitive Use

Nifty item. Farfetch'd has plenty of high critical hit ratio moves too, such as Slash, Night Slash, and Leaf Blade. It's even useful if you don't use these moves, as you still get a high chance of a critical hit. The downside? You have to use Farfetch'd. Who is about as competitive as Sunkern.
