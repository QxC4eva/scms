What is FU?

The idea behind FU is simple; any Pokémon with more than 2.28% or lower in the 1630 usage stats is unable to be used. This is a tier below the current existing PU tier, and is filled with its own variety of threats. This metagame won OMotM for January 2015 and currently has a ladder on Pokémon Showdown!


Rules
  • All Pokémon above 2.28% usage is unable to be used
  • All clauses are on.
  • Machoke, Sticky Web, Stoutland, and Vigoroth are all banned.

Threatlist

Electabuzz: Electabuzz carves itself a powerful niche in the FU metagame for being an insanely strong Electric-type in a tier where Electric is an excellent offensive typing. Electabuzz's high Speed and Special Attack, plus its good bulk with Eviolite, make it a force to be reckoned with. Between powerful Choice Specs Volt Switch and revenge killing Choice Scarf Thunderbolt, Electabuzz has the capacity to fill many roles and fulfill them well. Electabuzz's coverage hits everything it needs to, with Electric / Fighting / Ice, and they all have reasonably high power. Electabuzz was recently on suspect, but it was determined not broken and thus will stay in the tier.

Fraxure: Fraxure takes the cake for most powerful Pokémon in the tier, with a high base Attack stat of 117. This is on top of Dragon Dance, which skyrockets Fraxure's Attack stat to extremely high levels and boosts its average Speed stat. Fraxure is one of the only Dragon-types in the tier, and in a tier where Fairy-types are hard to come by no less! Fraxure is also an NFE, which means it can hold Eviolite and boosts its bulk to high levels. This bulk, along with its typing, allow it to set up on a large portion of the metagame. After a boost, Fraxure is quite hard to stop! Every team must be prepared for it.

Gogoat: Gogoat is quite possibly the most versatile Pokémon in the entire metagame. Between its high HP stat, good bulk and power, and an excellent ability in Sap sipper, Gogoat takes a bit to go down. A few roles Gogoat can fulfill are SubBU, defensive, Life Orb, 3 attacks + Milk Drink, and even some unconventional sets like Choice Band. Gogoat was another recently suspected Pokémon, but it was voted to stay in the tier because it's just a fantastic Pokémon but not quite banworthy. Nonetheless, it's a very versatile and powerful Pokémon that fits its way onto almost every team.
