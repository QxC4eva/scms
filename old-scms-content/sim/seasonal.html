[title] Pokemon Showdown Seasonal Ladder [head] [page]
By Joim.

Seasonal Ladder is a tier hosted on Pokemon Showdown. Much like Random Battle, the Seasonal Ladder lets you play with randomly generated teams; however, not every Pokemon can be randomly generated. The Pokemon that may be generated are based on themes relating to the time of the year. The current theme is Christmas Charade.

This month's theme is christmas. The Pokémon that may be found on this Seasonal are ice-types and christmas themed Pokémon. There's permanent hail to celebrate christmas. Additionally, all Pokémon use Present, which has a random effect, varying from powerful attacks to volatile statuses.
