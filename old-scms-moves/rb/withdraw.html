[SHORT DESC] Boosts Defense 1 stage. [LONG DESC]

Description

Boosts user's Defense one stage.

Competitive Use

Withdraw should never be considered on any competitive team. First of all, boosting defenses is a subpar strategy since the opponent can simply bring out a sweeper to boost alongside or attack with the other offensive stat. Second of all, there are moves that boost the target's Defense by two stages, meaning using those moves is much more efficient.

Related Moves

Harden is a Normal-type equivalent.

Defense Curl is a Normal-type equivalent.

Acid Armor raises Defense by two stages.

Barrier raises Defense by two stages.
