[SHORT DESC] 30% chance to flinch the target. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage and has a 30% chance to flinch the target.

Competitive Use

Mamoswine is the only common user of this move (the other user being Beartic), which is powerful and carries a 30% flinch chance. Icicle Crash is Mamoswine's best Ice-type move, and is a great choice for any set.

Related Moves

Ice Punch is a weaker option that may freeze.
