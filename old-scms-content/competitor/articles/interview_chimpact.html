Can you elaborate on the "stall" you used?
I used toxic spikes with Wish + Protect Spritzee and Fake Out Mienfoo if their team was weak to Toxic Spikes. I had Pumpkaboo to spinblock. Taunt Onix helped me with Defoggers.
Seems like a well built team.
Ah, that sounds pretty solid, especially considering how well you did. Are there any particular sets or teams that performed extraordinarily well for you during the tour?
Yea, the stall team did really well. I was also really happy with the teams that featured Ponyta or Larvesta.
A month ago he would have said he hated Little Cup. Did that change?
