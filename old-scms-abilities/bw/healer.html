[SHORT DESC] 30% chance to heal an adjacent teammate of a status condition every turn. [LONG DESC]

Description

In multi battles, the wielder has a 30% chance of removing status conditions from each adjacent ally at the end of every turn. If multiple Pokemon have Healer, each effect is applied separately.

Competitive Use

Healer is somewhat effective on Blissey and Audino in non-singles battles, but careful placement must be considered in order to heal both teammates in triple battles.
