[SHORT DESC] Raises Speed one stage when hit by a Bug-, Dark-, or Ghost-type move. [LONG DESC]

Description

When the wielder is hit by a Bug-, Dark-, or Ghost-type attack, its Speed is raised by one stage. A Speed boost will be granted for each hit of a multi-hit attack.

Competitive Use

Granbull prefers Intimidate, and Dunsparce has Serene Grace as its only gimmick. Sudowoodo really needs the Speed boosts, but is usually too frail to get fast enough before dying. In short, don't expect to see Rattled much competitively, as it is both situational and located on less than stellar Pokemon.
