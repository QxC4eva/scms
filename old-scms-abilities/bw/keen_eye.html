[SHORT DESC] The wielder's accuracy cannot be lowered. [LONG DESC]

Description

The wielder's accuracy cannot be lowered. Keen Eye does not block the effects of Haze or Heart Swap.

Competitive Use

Keen Eye is a poor ability as nobody uses accuracy reducing moves competitively. Therefore, the times that Keen Eye would even see use are very few. Always use a more consistent or reliable ability over Keen Eye.
