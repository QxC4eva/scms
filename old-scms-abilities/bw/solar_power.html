[SHORT DESC] HP decreases in the sun. Special Attack increases by 50%. [LONG DESC]

Description

While sun is the prevailing weather condition, the wielder's Special Attack is increased by 50% and it loses 1/8 of its maximum HP per turn.

Competitive Use

The one really good user of Solar Power is Charizard. You won't see it too much but when you do, you will know about it when you do. With Drought support from Ninetales, its STAB Fire-type attacks are monstrous. Other Pokemon such as Sunflora should never be used in OU anyway so the only example you will really find is Charizard.
