Description

Deals damage. Has an in-game effect that enables you to move big boulders.

Competitive Use

Even if throwing a giant boulder onto your opponent looks all powerful and mighty, this move should not be your first choice on a competitive team, since it lacks power and utility.

Related Moves

Body Slam is a more powerful alternative which boasts a 30% paralyze chance.

Double-Edge is a much stronger option with recoil.
