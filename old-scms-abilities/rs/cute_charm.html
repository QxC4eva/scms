[SHORT DESC] Can infatuate Pokémon of opposite gender when struck and increases their wild encounter rate. [LONG DESC]

Pokémon with this ability have a 30% chance of infatuating Pokémon of the opposite gender that use a contact move against it. If the attack does no damage, Cute Charm does not activate.

When a Pokémon with this ability is first in the party, wild encounters have a higher chance of being of the ability-bearer's opposite gender.
