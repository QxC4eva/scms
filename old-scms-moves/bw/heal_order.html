[SHORT DESC] Heals the user by 50% its max HP. [LONG DESC]

Description

Heals the user by 1/2 of its maximum HP, rounded half up.

Competitive Use

Vespiquen is lucky in that it gets not one, but two instant recovery moves in the form of Heal Order and Roost; both have different secondary effects. Roost is usually the better recovery move for Vespiquen as it softens up Rock-type attacks that can heavily dent or KO Vespiquen. However, if you hate losing the resistances Vespiquen's Flying typing brings when using Roost, Heal Order is a good alternative.

Related Moves

Recover is a Normal-type equivalent.

Slack Off is a Normal-type equivalent.

Softboiled is a Normal-type equivalent.

Roost is a Flying-type equivalent that grounds the user.

Milk Drink is a Normal-type equivalent.

Wish is an equivalent, but HP is recovered at the end of the second turn.

Moonlight and Morning Sun are equivalents, but vary in how much they heal depending on the weather.

Synthesis is a Grass-type equivalent, but varies in how much it heals depending on the weather.
