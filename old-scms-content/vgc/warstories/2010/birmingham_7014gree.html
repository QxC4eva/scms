Round 2

Next guy, he looks a bit older.

I get worried about Earthquake, then remember Electivire is crap.

I set up TR.

2-0

Round 3

From the moment I saw this guy and his friends, I knew he was a dick.

He sends out Jolteon + Togekiss.

Smeargle Detects, Jolteon fails to Thunder Wave it. Togekiss uses Aura Sphere on Dialga (~45%), Dialga sets up Trick Room.

Smeargle uses Dark Void, both fall asleep. Dialga Dragon Pulses Jolteon (~70%).

He starts complaining that I've violated the sleep clause. He gets the judges, they say play on :P.

I forget the rest from there, but I think he had a Metagross and Kyogre.

Mewtwo and Abomsnow clean up, 2-0.

Round 4 vs. Osirus182

So I had got through to the second set of battles, and was quite happy with my performance. Of course I wanted to go further :P

I go to the table and am facing a guy around 20ish. I say good luck, he says good luck. He sees my trainer name. He says something like "You're 7014gree?" I say yeah, he says something like he didn't want to battle me (correct me if I'm wrong).

I thought facing a Kyogre + Hitmontop lead was the worst possible outcome. It turns out Kyogre + Infernape is a lot worse.

Smeargle uses Follow Me, Infernape Fakes Out, Kyogre uses Water Spout(more than 50% to Dialga, Smeargle dies), Dialga sets up Trick Room.

Now this was the turning point in the match.

Out comes Abomasnow. I use Grass Knot on Kyogre for the OHKO, he switches Dialga in its place -__-. Dialga uses Dragon Pulse on Infernape, Focus Sash saves it. I thought Infernape would use Mach Punch, I completely forgot about Heat Wave. Dialga + Abomasnow are dead, Infernape dies from hail damage. He switches in Abomasnow, I switch in my last Pokemon, Mewtwo -__-.

Trick Room is still up, Mewtwo dies from Dragon Pulse + Blizzard before it can move. I lose 3-0.

If I'd predicted anything on the second turn, it could have been a completely different outcome, but he still won in the end; he was the more experienced player and he ended up losing in the third round and I hope he gets far in Hawaii. He beat some Belgian guy who I didn't like, so I don't hate him :P. I think he recorded the video, but I wouldn't be surprised if he deleted it :P. GG Osirus.

Although I only got to the top 32, it was a great experience and it was a fun day out.
