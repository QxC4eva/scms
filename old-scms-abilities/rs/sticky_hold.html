[SHORT DESC] This Pokémon's item cannot be removed. Increases the chance of encountering Pokémon while fishing. [LONG DESC]

If the enemy uses a move that would remove this Pokémon's item, such as Knock Off or Trick, it will not remove it.

When a Pokémon with this ability is first in the party, it is more likely that you will be prompted to enter battle against a wild Pokémon while fishing.
