[SHORT DESC] Has 30-70 power and can be any type but Normal, based on the user's DVs. [LONG DESC]

Description

The power of the move depends on the user's DVs, with the following formula:

Power = ((5 × X + Y) ÷ 2) + 31
  • To figure out X, you must use your Pokémon's DVs in the order of Attack / Defense / Speed / Special. One must convert each value; if the DV is equal to 7 or less, it gets converted to 0. If the DV is equal to 8 or more, it gets converted to 1. This four-digit number is called a "binary number." This is used below.

This four digit binary number is used to calculate X.

  • Y is the Special stat DV, but it is capped at 3, as shown in the table below.
Special DV Y Value
0 0
1 1
2 2
3 to 15 3

The final number is rounded down, and that is the power of your Hidden Power.

To work out the type, you have to use binary again. You must convert your Pokémon's Attack and Defense DVs into binary. The binary table above will do this for you, but instead, just swap the X value of 1 to 15 for the DVs 1 to 15, as the binary stays exactly the same. Then take the last two digits of the binary number from each value, in the order of Attack / Defense. This will then give you a four-digit number, and the types correspond with this number, as shown below.

Combine the Power and Type to get your full Hidden Power information.
