[SHORT DESC] 10% chance to flinch opponent. 10% chance to burn opponent. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage. Has a 10% chance to flinch the target. Has a 10% chance to burn the target.

Competitive Use

Fire Fang is less powerful and less accurate than other physical Fire-type moves such as Fire Punch and Flare Blitz. Therefore, it should only be used on Pokemon such as Garchomp that lack other Fire-type attacking options.

Related Moves

Fire Punch is a stronger alternative with 75 Base Power and 100% accuracy.

Flare Blitz is a stronger physical alternative with 120 Base Power, however it causes recoil damage.
