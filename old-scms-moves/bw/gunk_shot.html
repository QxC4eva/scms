[SHORT DESC] 30% chance to poison the target. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage and has a 30% chance to poison the target.

Competitive Use

Gunk Shot is not used much. Poison isn't prominent as an offensive type due to having poor super effective coverage; although powerful, this move also has only 70% accuracy. For these reasons Gunk Shot is exceedingly rare. Pokemon such as Arbok or Muk may sport it, as they need any extra power they can get. If in need of a Poison move to use, there are more reliable options across the board.

Related Moves

Poison Jab is a less powerful, but more accurate alternative.
