Now for last month's trivia answers!

  1. Whose gym involves pushing boulders into lava rivers to form bridges?
    Clair
  2. What HMs do you use in the Distortion World?
    Both Surf and Strength
  3. Name one item available in the 2nd generation games that is completely unavailable in later games.
    There are several answers for this question. Some popular correct answers include the Berserk Gene and the Berries from the 2nd Generation games. Several people submitted Sacred Ash as an answer, however, this is incorrect! Sacred Ash is available through an event in 3rd Generation games that allows you to battle Ho-Oh and Lugia.
  4. Who is the Gym Leader of Cianwood City?
    Chuck
  5. Which Pokémon, besides Shedinja, takes the least damage from Meteor Mash with 252 HP / 252 Def EVs?
    Empoleon
  6. What is the lightest fully evolved Pokémon?
    Castform at a meagre 1.8 Pounds
  7. Which move has the lowest priority?
    Trick Room
  8. What Pokémon has never left OU since RBY?
    Snorlax
  9. What is the only un-typed attack?
    Curse
  10. Which TM from GSC is still a TM in later games?
    Sandstorm
