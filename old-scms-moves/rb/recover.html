[SHORT DESC] Heals 50% of user's max HP. [LONG DESC]

Description

Recovers 50% of the user's max HP. Recover will fail if the user's HP is full, has 511 below max HP, 255 below max HP, or no HP.

Competitive Use

Recover is one of the best moves in the game, and it, or its equivalent Softboiled, is almost mandatory for any defensive Pokemon and even some offensive ones. Alakazam and Starmie are the primary users of this move, walling special attacks, and even physical attacks with Reflect in the case of the first, recovering all damage received. Generally, if a Pokemon has access to Recover, it should be used.

Related Moves

Softboiled is an exact equivalent.

Rest heals the user's full health and status but puts it to sleep for two turns.
