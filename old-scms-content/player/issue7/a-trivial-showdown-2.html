Welcome to the second installment of A Trivial Showdown! This will run similarly to The Smog's puzzle page. Basically, just answer as many of the questions given below as you can, and once you think you've got them, PM your solutions to The Player on Smogon. Correctly answered Easy questions are worth 1 point, Medium questions are worth 2, Hard questions are worth 3, and the Bonus question is worth 5 points. We'll announce the winners (the three people with the most points) when the next issue of The Player rolls around. Prizes will be given out to the winners. Good luck and have fun!

Puzzles

Question: Hint

Easy - Hangman. Fill out the rest of the word according to the hint.

  1. _ _ o _ _ _ _ _ _ _: PS global staff member.
  2. _ _ _ _ _ _ o _: PS public room.
  3. _ _ a _ _ a _ _ _: User in the PS credits.
  4. _ _ _ _ _ _ _ v _ _: Former PS global Moderator.
  5. _ _ _ _ _ m _ _ _: PS public room Owner.

Medium - Anagrams. Switch around the order of the letters to receive a PS-related answer (ex. Need Hug Gun would be Hugendugen)

  1. Plasma Sin Trots: PS global staff member and a public room he/she owns.
  2. Ran Biz Eek: PS global Mod and Smogon Super Mod.
  3. Smarten Unto: Tied with two other public rooms for the most room Owners.
  4. Castrate Prism: PS global staff member in charge of move animations.

Hard - "Psywaves" Questions. Use the clues to find synonyms to complete the full word. Be careful because this type of question is based on pronunciation. For example, when a clue is "A Letter" the letter may be "R" but be spelled "ar" in the word. (ex. "Onomatopoeia + Hole + A" Letter would be Pupitar. Pew + Pit + Ar)

  1. Letter + Letter + Reserve + Smooth + Layers: PS public room.
  2. Letter + Letter + Bird + Letter + Shape: PS public room Owner.
  3. Body Part + Letter + No: PS global staff member.
