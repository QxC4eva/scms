[SHORT DESC] Boosts experience points 50% when held. [LONG DESC]

Description

When held by a Pokémon who participated in battle, the Exp. Points awarded to that Pokémon are multiplied by 1.5.

Location

A Lucky Egg is given to you by Professor Juniper in Chargestone Cave during the main story. They can also be found, albeit very rarely using a Pokemon of level 41-60 with the ability Pickup, and the Treasure Hunter on Route 13 will sometimes yield Lucky Eggs.
