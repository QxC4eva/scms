[SHORT DESC] 50% chance to lower target's Defense by 1 stage. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage and has a 50% chance to lower the target's Defense by one stage.

Competitive Use

This move has too little power and a secondary effect that is not reliable enough to be remotely competitive.

Related Moves

Close Combat is a competitive alternative with 120 Base Power but lowers the user's Defense and Special Defense by one stage.

Superpower is a competitive alternative with 120 Base Power, but lowers the user's Attack and Defense one stage.

Brick Break is an alternative with 75 Base Power that can also destroy Reflect and Light Screen.
