[SHORT DESC] Doubles damage on resisted hits. [LONG DESC]

Description

When the wielder uses a move that is not very effective on its target, the damage is doubled.

Competitive Use

Tinted Lens is a great ability, and it's a shame it is only limited at the moment to some quite shockingly bad Pokemon. The main users at the moment in OU are Yanmega and Venomoth, both Bug-types, but neither really able to make a lasting impression in that tier. Both of these are monstrous threats in the lower tiers, but in the OU metagame, Tinted Lens is an ability you very rarely see.
