[SHORT DESC] This Pokémon is immune to Ground-type moves. [LONG DESC]

This Pokémon is immune to all Ground-type attacks, Spikes, and Toxic Spikes, and can switch out of Arena Trap. These immunities are lost while under the effects of Gravity or Ingrain, or while holding Iron Ball. Roost does not cause the user to lose Levitate during the turn it is used.
