Let it Snow: Part Two!

UU's first suspect test has come to a close, and the very first extended Senate has voted on the tiering of Snow Warning. With ten votes for unbanning the ability (and one abstain), Snow Warning is once again legal in the UU metagame. ~kokoloko

Let it Snow!

The first suspect retest of UU is underway! Following a decision by the UU Senate, the UU community is testing the ability Snow Warning to see if Hail is still broken in UU. The UU Suspect Ladder is located on Pokemon Showdown and a new megathread has been created to discuss Hail's impact on the current UU metagame. A formal vote will be held by the Senate after November 24th. ~kokoloko

October Tier Shifts

Four new Pokemon are now UU, and no Pokemon rose up to OU. Two Pokemon who used to be UU are now back at home: Mew, who was considered for suspect numerous times, and Tornadus, who was considered mediocre by quite a few people. In addition to the previously UU Pokemon, two brand new Pokemon are now UU: Virizion and Meloetta. This is an interesting shake up to the UU metagame. Will Mew still be the beast it always was? Is Tornadus still mediocre without its permanent rain? Will the speculation about Meloetta and Virizion being average turn out to be true? Contribute to the discussion here and the megathread here. ~DTC and kokoloko
