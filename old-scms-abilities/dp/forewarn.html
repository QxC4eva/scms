[SHORT DESC] Tells which of the opponent's moves has the highest Base Power. [LONG DESC]

Alerts the user to the opponent's move that has the highest Base Power when the user switches in. If two moves are of the same Base Power, Forewarn randomly selects one. In Double Battles, Forewarn will only reveal one move total, not one move from each opponent. It selects the highest power move from the two opponents. All moves without a Base Power are assigned a power.

Assigned Base Power Moves
160 Fissure, Guillotine, Horn Drill, and Sheer Cold
150 Eruption, Water Spout
120 Counter, Metal Burst, and Mirror Coat
80 Crush Grip, Dragon Rage, Endeavor, Flail, Frustration, Grass Knot, Gyro Ball, Hidden Power, Low Kick, Natural Gift, Night Shade, Psywave, Return, Reversal, Seismic Toss, SonicBoom, Trump Card, and Wring Out
0 Everything without a fixed Base Power not listed above.
