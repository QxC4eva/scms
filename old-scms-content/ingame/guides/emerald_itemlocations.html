List of Competitve TMs/HMs

The following is a list of all competitive TMs obtainable in Pokemon Emerald. Any TMs that lack competitive use, whether it be due to a low Base Power or other reason, have been excluded.

TM01 (Focus Punch): Route 115
To reach TM01, you'll have to head to the northern part of the mountain, accessed easiest via Surfing from the southern end of the Route. The item is up the mountain path, to the left of a rock directly north of the path.
TM02 (Dragon Claw): Meteor Falls
Go to the back room in the basement of Meteor Falls. To get there, you'll need Surf and Waterfall. TM02 is located on the northern shore, lying on the ground.
TM04 (Calm Mind): Mossdeep City
Defeat Mossdeep City's Gym Leaders, Tate and Liza.
TM05 (Roar): Route 114
Before you reach the bridge that takes you to the southern area of the Route, talk to the Gentleman with a Poochyena beside him.
TM06 (Toxic): Fiery Path
TM06 is found in the northeastern corner of Fiery Path, in the area accessible only by Strength.
TM08 (Bulk Up): Dewford City
Defeat Dewford City's Gym Leader, Brawly.
TM10 (Hidden Power): Fortree City
Go in the middle house in the top section of Fortree City. Inside, talk to the old lady and correctly guess in which hand she's hidden the coin. It can also be bought in the Slateport City market for $1000.
TM11 (Sunny Day): Scorched Slab
To get to TM11, you'll need Surf. It is located on the northern shore, lying on the ground.
TM12 (Taunt): Trick House
Awarded as a prize for completing the fifth challenge.
TM13 (Ice Beam): Abandoned Ship
In the locked room on the basement floor, needs the Storage key to unlock it. TM 13 can also be received in exchange for 4000 coins in the Mauville Game Corner from the cashier on the left.
TM14 (Blizzard): Lilycove Department Store
Can be purchased for 5500 from the left hand cashier on the fourth floor.
TM15 (Hyper Beam): Lilycove Department Store
Can be purchased for 7500 from the left hand cashier on the fourth floor.
TM16 (Light Screen): Lilycove Department Store
Can be purchased for 3000 from the right hand cashier on the fourth floor.
TM17 (Protect): Lilycove Department Store
Can be purchased for 3000 from the right hand cashier on the fourth floor.
TM18 (Rain Dance): Abandoned Ship
Dive into the dark patch of water located in one of the rooms on the basement floor of the Abandoned Ship. Resurface, and head to Room 1 (the first door adjacent to the spot you resurface from) to find TM 18.
TM19 (Giga Drain): Route 123
TM19 is given to you by an NPC, found at the end of the peninsula. To get there, you need to Surf around Mt. Pyre.
TM20 (Safeguard): Lilycove Department Store
Can be purchased for $3000 from the right hand cashier on the fourth floor.
TM21 (Frustration): Pacifidlog Town
Talk to the man in the house on the lower left with an "unhappy" Pokemon at the front of your party.
TM22 (SolarBeam): Safari Zone
Surf across a small body of water—located in an area that can only be reached using the Mach Bike—to find TM 22.
TM24 (Thunderbolt): Mauville City
Given to you by Gym Leader Wattson after completing his side-quest in New Mauville. Can also be bought in the Mauville Game Corner for 4000 coins from the cashier on the left.
TM25 (Thunder): Lilycove Department Store
Can be bought for 5500 from the left hand cashier on the fourth floor.
TM26 (Earthquake): Seafloor Cavern
Located in one the chambers you pass through while heading to the deeper parts of the cave (Dive, Rock Smash, Strength, and Surf are needed to reach and navigate through the Cavern). TM 26 can also be obtained from Pokemon with the Pickup ability.
TM27 (Return): Fallarbor Town, Pacifidlog Town
Talk to Prof. Cozmo in Fallarbor Town once you've obtained the meteorite and he will give you the TM in exchange for it. Talk to the man in the house on the lower left of Pacifidlog Town with a "happy" Pokemon at the front of your party to obtain the TM.
TM28 (Dig): Fossil Maniac's House
Walk all the way to the back of the house, go through the cave, and talk to the Fossil Maniac.
TM29 (Psychic): Mauville Game Corner
Can be purchased for 3500 coins from the cashier on the left.
TM30 (Shadow Ball): Mt. Pyre
TM30 is found on the sixth floor of Mt. Pyre, lying on the ground. You need Surf to access Mt. Pyre.
TM31 (Brick Break): Sootopolis City
Given to you by the man living on the left side of Sootopolis City, in the square house northwest of the PokeMart. You need Surf to access the left side of Sootopolis.
TM32 (Double Team): Route 113
Northwest corner by the ninja boy in the ash pile. Can also be obtained in the Mauville Game Corner in exchange for 1500 from the cashier on the left.
TM33 (Reflect): Lilycove Department Store
Can be purchased for 3000 from the right hand cashier on the fourth floor.
TM35 (Flamethrower): Mauville Game Corner
Can be exchanged for 4000 coins from the cashier on the left.
TM36 (Sludge Bomb): Dewford Town
Given to you by the little girl in the building in the northwestern part of town.
TM38 (Fire Blast): Lilycove Department Store
Can be purchased for 5500 from the left hand cashier on the fourth floor.
TM40 (Aerial Ace): Fortree City
Defeat Fortree City's Gym Leader, Winona.
TM41 (Torment): Slateport City
Talk to the Sailor in the Battle Tent, located in the northwestern corner of town.
TM42 (Facade): Petalburg City
Defeat Petalburg City's Gym Leader, Norman.
TM44 (Rest): Lilycove City
Given to you by the man in the house directly right of the Move Deleter's home. Can also be obtained by Pokemon with the Pickup ability.
TM50 (Overheat): Lavaridge Town
Defeat Lavaridge Town's Gym Leader, Flannery.
HM01 (Cut): Rustboro City
Talk to the man inside the Cutter's House, to the left of the Pokemon Center.
HM02 (Fly): Route 119
Defeat your rival after completing the Weather Institute side-quest.
HM03 (Surf): Petalburg City
Talk to Wally's uncle after beating Norman. He is located in the northwestern house, left of the Gym.
HM04 (Strength): Rusturf Tunnel
Given to you by a Black Belt after using Rock Smash to clear the rocks from the middle of Rusturf Tunnel.
HM05 (Flash): Granite Cave
Given to you by the Hiker on the first floor of the cave.
HM06 (Rock Smash): Mauville City
Talk to the Scientist in the house located to the right of the Pokemon Center.
HM07 (Waterfall): Sootopolis City
Given to you by Wallace after awakening Rayquaza.
HM08 (Dive): Mossdeep City
Given to you as a present from Steven.

List of Move Tutors

The following is a list of every move tutor available in Pokemon Emerald. Each move tutor, except for the Battle Frontier move tutors, can only teach one of your Pokemon their respective move.

Double-Edge
In Sootopolis City's Pokemon Center is a trainer who will teach your Pokemon the move Double-Edge.
DynamicPunch
Near Mossdeep City's entrance is a trainer who will be willing to teach your Pokemon the move DynamicPunch.
Explosion
In Pacifidlog Town's Pokemon Center is a tutor who will teach the move Explosion to your Pokemon.
Fury Cutter
In Verdanturf Town's Pokemon Center is a kid who will teach your Pokemon the move Fury Cutter.
Metronome
In Fallarbor Town's PokeMart is a woman who will teach your Pokemon the fancy move Metronome.
Mimic
Found in Lavaridge Town is a small boy who will teach your Pokemon the move Mimic.
Rollout
Near Mauville City's Gym is a guy who will teach your Pokemon the move Rollout.
Sleep Talk
In Fortree City is an old man who will teach your Pokemon the move Sleep Talk.
Substitute
On the roof of Lilycove City's Dept. Store is a trainer who will teach your Pokemon the move Substitute.
Swagger
In Slateport City's Fan Club is a tutor who will teach your Pokemon the move Swagger.
Move Tutor
In Fallarbor Town, there is a man who will teach any of your Pokemon any move that they have forgotten at an expense of one Heart Scale per move.
The Battle Frontier's Tutors
After beating the Elite Four and unlocking the Battle Frontier, you will find a house near the Battle Arena with two tutors who will both be able to teach your Pokemon some moves at cost of some Battle Points.
