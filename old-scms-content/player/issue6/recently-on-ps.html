Latest News

Trivia Script

By Dell on December 10, 2014

Thanks to Morfent's hard work, the Trivia room now has a script! Featuring three different modes of gameplay, everyone is welcome to answer questions, join games, and climb up the trivia leaderboard. Subjects for questions range from pop culture to Pokemon to academic subjects. Enjoy!


New: Unfortunately, as soon as the script was proving successful, it struck some kinks that have yet to be worked out. As of the end of December, Morfent is working hard at fixing the script so the room doesn't have to remain reverted to users asking questions and the normal slower pace that was present before the script.

VGC 2015

By Dell on December 17, 2014

VGC 2015 rules have been released, including its own format! VGC 2014 will coexist with the new format until the season ends (December 31st!), so you guys can still duke it out in 2014's format until then. VGC 2015's rules are very similar to flat double battle rules; most 600+ BST legendaries and the Soul Dew are banned. Check it out, and head over to the VGC room for dedicated tournaments and discussion!

PS! TVBF Oscars

By Dell on December 24, 2014

The TV, Books and Films room is holding an award ceremony to celebrate all the great movie releases this year! Vote for your movie of choice in each of 18 different categories, including cinematography, original song and director, to name a few! This event will be held at 5 PM EST on December 30, so head on over there and enjoy the ceremony!


New: The ceremony was held last night and was an absolute success. Some of the Oscar winners include Guardians of the Galaxy for best Action/Adventure film, and Everything is Awesome from The Lego Movie for Best Original Song. If you want to see the full list of winners, click here. In the future, why don't you join in the fun that the TV, Books and Film room has to offer? I'm telling you, it's worth it.

BLT: Best 'Leaderboarders' Tournament

By Dell on December 29, 2014

The Tournaments room will be hosting the Best 'Leaderboarders' Tournament, where the winners of the scripted tournaments in the room will be ranked given points, and will come together in April for a big team based tournament! Check out this link for more details, and we hope to see you in the room earning points!

The managers are excited to see what potential competition will rise out of the top leaderboard players, so be sure to give each tournament everything you've got. Even if you don't think you'll make it into the top set of players, every team will be pruning an additional member from anyone that scored in at least a bare minimum amount of tournaments, so even a few wins could land you a spot on a draft. Get out there and show us your best!
