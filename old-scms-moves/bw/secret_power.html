[SHORT DESC] Effects vary with terrain. 30% chance to lower target's accuracy by 1 stage in Wi-Fi battles. [LONG DESC]

Deals damage. Gains a 30% chance of causing an effect, depending on the terrain it is used in. These are as follows:

Building
Paralysis
Cave/Rock
Flinch
Grass
Sleep
Puddle
Lowers Speed by one stage
Sand/Plain
Lowers accuracy by one stage
Snow
Freezes
Water
Lowers Attack by one stage

In Wi-Fi and simulator battles, the terrain is always assumed as "Plan/Sand." Although a physical move, this move does not make contact with the opponent.

Competitive Use

Secret Power has little competitive use. The chance to lower accuracy is also not a big draw, since doing so is an unreliable tactic. The only slightly viable users of the move would be Normal-types, due to the fact hey receive STAB damage on it. However, as far as most of them are concerned, they definitely has better things that it could be doing with the moveslot.

Related Moves

Return and Frustration are both more powerful alternatives.
