Description

Deals damage and has a 10% chance to paralyze the target.

Competitive Use

Despite its raw power, Thunder sports poor accuracy, nearly all players opt to choose the more reliable, and still powerful, Thunderbolt instead. Using Thunder is too risky in an already luck based metagame.

Related Moves

Thunderbolt is a less powerful albeit more reliable alternative.
