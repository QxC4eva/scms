[SHORT DESC] 10% chance to paralyze. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage and has a 10% chance to paralyze the target.

Competitive Use

While this move is great for defeating Misty's Starmie, it isn't useful for much else, no matter how good Pikachu makes it look. Ditch ThunderShock as soon as you have access to Thunderbolt or any other Electric-type move that deals decent damage.

Related Moves

Thunderbolt is a more powerful alternative.

Thunder is a much more powerful but less accurate alternative.
