Welcome to the third installment of A Trivial Showdown! Basically, answer as many of the questions given below as you can, and once you think you've got them, PM your solutions to The Player on Smogon. Correctly answered Easy questions are worth 1 point, Medium questions are worth 2 points, Hard questions are worth 3 points, and the Bonus question is worth 5 points. We'll announce the winners (the three people with the most points) when the next issue of The Player rolls around. Prizes will be given out to the winners. Good luck and have fun!

Puzzles

Question: Hint

Easy - Hangman. Fill out the rest of the word according to the hint.

  1. _ _ a _ _ _ _ _: PS global staff member.
  2. _ _ _ _ _ _ a _ _ _ _: PS public room.
  3. _ _ a _ _ _ _ _ _: Former PS global Mod.
  4. _ _ _ a _ _ _ _ _ _: PS public room Owner.
  5. _ _ _ a _ _ _ _ _: Former PS Leader/Admin.

Medium - Anagrams. Switch around the order of the letters to receive a PS-related answer (ex. Need Hug Gun would be Hugendugen).

  1. A Repent Blob Flutist: PS user and a room he/she owns.
  2. Tent Glob Dots: PS user and a room he/she is a Mod in.
  3. Luck Lie Due: Pokémon and the ladder on which it was #1 in usage in December 2014.
  4. Airplane Bed: PS global Mod and a Pokémon in his/her custom avatar.

Hard - "Psywaves" Questions. Use the clues to find synonyms to complete the full word. Be careful because this type of question is based on pronunciation. For example, when a clue is "A Letter" the letter may be "R" but be spelled "ar" in the word. (ex. "Onomatopoeia + Hole + A" Letter would be Pupitar. Pew + Pit + Ar)

  1. Street + Hill + Ones: Battle format on PS.
  2. Two + Preposition + Religious symbol: Former PS global Mod.
  3. Limit + Expert + Throw: PS public room.

Bonus Question!

  • Take the only PS global Mod whose username starts with a "W" and find the first thread he posted in on Smogon. Find the Pokémon in the user's avatar that posted 4 posts after his in the same thread. This Pokémon was another user's old PS username. Take this user's global rank number (Voice = 1 Driver = 2 etc.) and multiply it by the national dex number of his/her favorite Pokémon. Take this number and subtract from it Smogon user Phizzlax's user number. Find the Pokémon that has this national dex number and the final answer is the PS user has that Pokémon in his/her custom avatar.
