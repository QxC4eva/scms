[SHORT DESC] Blocks evasion modifiers. Allows Fighting- and Normal-type moves to hit Ghosts. [LONG DESC]

Nullifies all positive evasion modifiers, both present and future, on the target. Foresight doesn't nullify the evasion increase of items or abilities. Fighting- and Normal-type moves can hit Ghosts under the effect of Foresight.

Related Moves

Odor Sleuth is identical.
