[title] Monthly Art Contest #4: Dragon's Den [head] [page]

A hallowed place, full of wonder. Supposedly only masters of the Dragon-type can enter. And, of course, any random 10-year-old who happens to defeat Clair. Still, the Dragon's Den of Johto has always been a location shrouded in mystery through both its secretive nature and the mysticism of the type it represents. From what better, then, is there to draw inspiration? There were a variety of interpretations, some of which more creative than others (that is: Shinxe...).
