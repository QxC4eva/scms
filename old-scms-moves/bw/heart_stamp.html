[SHORT DESC] 30% chance to flinch the target. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage and has a 30% chance to flinch the target.

Competitive Use

Terrible distribution and low Base Power on a move with bad offensive typing make Heart Stamp an awful option. Stick with Zen Headbutt if you want a good physical Psychic-type attack.

Related Moves

Psycho Cut is a slightly more powerful option with a high critical hit ratio.

Zen Headbutt is a more powerful but less accurate option with a lower flinch rate.

Bite is a Dark-type equivalent.
