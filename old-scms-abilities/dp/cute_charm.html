[SHORT DESC] Can infatuate Pokémon of opposite gender when struck. Higher chance of encountering Pokémon of the opposite gender. [LONG DESC]

This Pokémon has a 30% chance of infatuating Pokémon of the opposite gender who attack with a contact move. If the attack does no damage, Cute Charm does not activate.

When a Pokémon with this ability is first in the party, wild Pokémon have a higher chance of being of the opposite gender of this Pokemon.
