Description

Deals damage and has a 10% chance to freeze the target.

Competitive Use

The second half of the acclaimed BoltBeam combination, Ice Beam is powerful and perfectly accurate. It is freely available as a TM, thus allowing many Pokemon, most notably Water-types, to learn it. Very good move that should be used often.
