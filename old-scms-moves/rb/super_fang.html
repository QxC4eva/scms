[SHORT DESC] Does damage equal to half the foe's current HP. [LONG DESC]

Description

Does damage equal to half of the target's current HP. This move can hit Ghost-types.

Competitive Use

Super Fang is a helpful attack for Pokemon that have a hard time muscling through tougher walls and lack better forms of coverage. The best example around is Raticate, who can then use Hyper Beam to finish off the opposing Pokemon.
