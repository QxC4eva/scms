[SHORT DESC] Increases the chance that the opponent will flinch when the wielder attacks. Decreases wild encounter rate. [LONG DESC]

Description

The wielder has a 10% chance of flinching the opponent whenever it attacks. This does not stack with King's Rock or Razor Fang. In-game, Stench decreases wild encounter rate.

Competitive Use

Stench really isn't that viable, but can be good in a pinch if the luck is going for you. Garbodor, Skuntank, and Muk are three candidates for using Stench but the former two have a much better ability in Aftermath, and the latter is too slow to effectively make use of it.
