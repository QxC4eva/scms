Thursday

Came over, drove around, and visited with the fam.

Friday

We got out of our temp hotel fairly early, and drove around my camp and the VGC place.

At the hotel, I was like, the first Pokemon guy to check in. I see some EV trainers, say hi, and connect Pokewalkers, and they went to fix up their room and I didn't see them after that. I'm starting to miss Dimsunlight, but then Noxe shows up at the bar area with another dude, (what was his name?) and I plopped my little Groudon plush next to his DSiXL, and he lit up, saying, "That's my man!" and we were soon at it, battling our monsters. I recognized his game name, Noxe, and I said, "You're Noxe?" "Yeah! How do you know that?" "I'm lucariojr on Skarmbliss!" and the battle continued, leading to a 2-1 defeat for me, he used Explosion on Scarfed ogre, and got a crit Night Slash, but it was a pretty good game (he commented on Latios surviving Blizzard, though). Then some Juniors came in and started Multi battling, and challenged me, and I went to Noxe and said they challenged US (that was a white lie, sorry! :D) so we went in and beat them easily (even considering that I didn't have all lv.100's like Noxe), but both sides had fun with it. Like Ostrigal said, these guys play from the heart.

Noxe went back upstairs and I didn't see him anymore, but I stayed down and chilled with the juniors, and one new senior (didn't EV train, but had some potential, IMO), had some more multi battles, showed off my Groudon plush some, and talked with their parents. I was about to go back to my room, but then Marrilad and that announcer guy. Announcer didn't pay me any attention, but Marrilad stopped to connect Pokewalkers with me, and I took my picture with him, and dad talked with him a little (awesome!). Ready for tomorrow!

…But not after about 2 more hours of non-stop Pokemon. Cleaned out three stragglers, watched some battle vids with Noxe's group… Then bed at 12:30-1:00 AM.

Now ready for tomorrow!

ROUND ONE

Oh man, not a noob. Why can't I get the noobs? Geez. Anyway, He hit the field with Toxicroak and Kyogre, and I send out Kustard and Octopus. I recall Kustard, as I smell a Fake Out/Water Spout coming my way, I send Morgan out, he tries to Sucker Punch Octo, but it fails due to TR, and both Morgan and Octopus are still standing with HP to spare. He recalls ogre and sends Palkia out, I proceed to use Shadow Sneak on the new Palkia, and Toxicroak decides he BADLY wants to hit Octo with Sucker Punch. "But it failed!" He groans, and both his mons take a Rock Slide, and puts Palkia under 50%. I recall Morgan, thinking ogre would come back, it did, and Palkia takes another Shadow Sneak, and then Palkia Dives. ("It's worked before!") I switch Octo out to Morgan, Follow Me with Kustard, and ogre Spouts, killing Morgan and leaving Kustard to be killed by Dive. CARL and Octo come out again, I Rock Slide and get a crit on Palkia, and I fire off a Draco Meteor on ogre. Toxicroak comes back, ogre switches with Registeel, only to take a boosted Flamethrower to the face, and Octo sets up TR again. Steel protects, I Shadow Sneak Croak, rendering another Sucker Punch attempt futile. Ogre comes back to weaken a Flamethrower aimed at Steel, but is KO'd by Shadow Sneak. A few turns later the match is over with me moving on to round two. (2-0)

ROUND TWO

I thought she was a serious battler. Then she used Psybeam. (4-0)
