[SHORT DESC] A Ball used to catch Pokemon in the Entralink forest. [LONG DESC]

Description

This Ball can only be used in Entralink forest, to catch Pokemon that have Dream World abilities.

Competitive Use

This item is useful only for catching Pokemon with Dream World abilities, such as Regenerator Slowbro or Multiscale Dragonite. Other than that, it is useless competitively.

Location

Entralink Forest.
