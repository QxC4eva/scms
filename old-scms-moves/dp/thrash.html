[SHORT DESC] Repeats for 2-3 turns. Confuses the user. [LONG DESC]

Locks the user into this move for 2-3 turns. Confuses the user at the end.

Related Moves

Petal Dance is a Grass-type equivalent.
Outrage is a Dragon-type equivalent that has a higher Base Power.
Uproar is an equivalent that has a lower Base Power and prevents any Pokemon in play from falling asleep.
