[SHORT DESC] Unaffected by sound moves. [LONG DESC]

This Pokémon is unaffected by all sound moves, which include GrassWhistle, Growl, Hyper Voice, Metal Sound, Perish Song, Roar, Sing, SonicBoom, Supersonic, Screech, Snore, Uproar, and Heal Bell. If Perish Song is used on the Soundproof Pokémon and the Soundproof Pokémon uses Baton Pass, Perish Song still takes effect on the recipient.
