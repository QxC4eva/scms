Rapid Spinning Among the Tiers

For Defog:

Apart from Rapid Spin, the newly-buffed Defog now provides another way to get rid of entry hazards. Defog clears both the user and the opponent's side of the field of all entry hazards, Reflect and Light Screen, and lowers the opponent's evasiveness. This is extremely useful because there is no guaranteed way to block Defog. This allows offensive teams to utilize powerful Pokémon such as Mega Pinsir and Charizard, both of which are weak to Stealth Rock, by instantly clearing the field. Stall teams are also able to switch more freely with the help of Defog without fearing entry hazards. Methods of discouraging Defog include using Defiant users such as Bisharp and Thundurus; however, teams with Defog often carry ways of beating those threats. As such, there is no guaranteed way of keeping entry hazards on the field with Defog's introduction. Common users of Defog in OU include Latias, Mandibuzz, and Mega Scizor.

Alternative Metagame Articles and Help

Smogon is home to a huge variety of metagames which are still developing for Generation 6 (XY). Currently, Smogon supports Official Tiers for Ubers, OU (Over Used), UU (Under Used), RU (Rarely Used), NU (Never Used), LC (Little Cup), Doubles, and VGC (Video Games Championship, Nintendo's own metagame).

Alternatively, Pokémon Showdown (as well as Smogon) supports "Other Metagames," which stem from either the game (Inverse Battles, Sky Battles) or from peoples own ideas (OffStat, STABMons) which modify the games properties and can lead to very different gaming experiences. These can be found in the Other Metagames forum here.

As a final thought, the people over at the Create-A-Pokémon Project also have their own ladder which doesn't really fit into any of the above categories. CaP, as it is commonly known, creates Pokémon which do battle across the tier of OU and attempts to understand and learn about the metagame.
