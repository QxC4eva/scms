Introduction

This article contains a list of all the pre-generated Trainers you may face on the Battle Subway in Pokemon Black and White / Black 2 and White 2 Versions, as well as a cross-referenced pool of the generated Pokemon sets they individually use. As such, this list is designed to be used alongside the Battle Subway Pokemon article, and it won't be useful without it. While it may seem daunting at first glance, it is actually quite simply designed, and will prove to be an invaluable resource for those looking to master the Battle Subway.

To deduce which Pokemon sets the opponent may be using, find the name of the Trainer in question and cross-reference the numbers (which link directly to their respective entries for your convenience) with the Battle Subway Pokemon article. These numbers correspond to the 986 Pokemon sets in the Battle Subway Pokemon list. This list provides information on their moveset, nature, hold item, and EV spread. Of course, some Trainers may use multiple sets of the same Pokemon, which means you might not be able to determine the exact variation immediately, but in many cases a single attack should be enough to narrow down the possibilities significantly.
