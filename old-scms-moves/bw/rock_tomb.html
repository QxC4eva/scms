[SHORT DESC] Lowers target's Speed by 1 stage. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage and has a 100% chance to lower the target's Speed by one stage.

Competitive Use

Decreasing the Speed of the opponent with a damaging move can be a slightly compelling reason to use a move as weak as Rock Tomb. However, like all moves that temporarily reduce the opponent's stats, it is rather unreliable and the payoff is not worth the reduced power.

Related Moves

Rock Slide is a stronger alternative with 75 Base Power and 30% chance to flinch the opponent.

Stone Edge is a stronger alternative with 100 Base Power and 80% accuracy.

Low Sweep is a slightly stronger Fighting-type equivalent.
