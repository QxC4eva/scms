[SHORT DESC] Reduces damage from a super effective Psychic-type attack by 50%. Consumed after use. [LONG DESC]

When held by a Pokemon, reduces the power of a damaging Psychic-type move that would hit the holder for super effective damage by 50%. Consumed after use. Natural Gift becomes a 60 Base Power Psychic-type attack when the user holds this Berry, but the Berry is consumed after use.

Competitive Use

This item, though it has a competitive function, sees very little competitive use because Psychic is not a commonly used type of attack, and most Fighting- and Poison-type Pokemon cannot afford to relinquish their item slot to use Payapa Berry.
