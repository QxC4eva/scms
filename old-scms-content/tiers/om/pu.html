What is PU?

PU is a hypothetical tier underneath NU that features any Pokémon with a usage cutoff of 3.406% or lower. This name does not mean Poorly Used, Partially Used, or Potentially Used; it is a play on words that references to "pee-you!", a noise something makes when another stinks. PU is quite the popular tier and has it's own room on Pokémon Showdown! that can be found here, an IRC channel you can find here, or you can discuss it in the main thread here. There's a variety of different ways to find discussion on PU! The viability thread can be found here!

Rules:

  • Endless Battle Clause: Players cannot use any moveset on any Pokémon capable of intentionally causing an endless battle. Thus: A Pokémon may not carry Recycle and hold a Leppa Berry in conjunction with Heal Pulse and Milk Drink, Moonlight, Morning Sun, Recover, Roost, Slack Off, Soft-Boiled, or Wish. A Pokémon may not hold a Leppa Berry while carrying Recycle and Pain Split. A Pokémon may not hold a Leppa Berry while carrying Recycle and Fling.
  • Evasion Clause: Players cannot use the moves Double Team or or Minimize.
  • Moody Clause: Players cannot use a Pokémon with the Moody ability.
  • OHKO Clause: Players cannot use the moves Fissure, Guillotine, Horn Drill, or Sheer Cold.
  • Sleep Clause: Players cannot induce sleep to more than one Pokémon on the opposing team at the same time.
  • Species Clause: Players cannot have two Pokémon with the same Pokédex number on the same team.
  • Swagger Clause: Players cannot use the move Swagger.
  • Baton Pass Clause: Players cannot have more than one Pokémon with the move Baton Pass.
  • Musharna and Garbodor are banned as well.
  • Glalite, Lopunnite, Beedrillinite are banned.

Threat List

Carracosta: Carracosta is a very large threat thanks to its access to the incredibly potent move Shell Smash, instantly boosting its offenses and Speed to threatening levels. Furthermore, Carracosta has reliable bulk and a solid ability which allow it the luxury of an easy setup in most cases. Carracosta is also quite unpredictable in its coverage, having an expansive selection of moves from both the physical and special spectrum. Surprisingly, Carracosta can even pull of a fantastic defensive set with its high bulk! Carracosta is a top threat in PU that every team must account for.
Poliwrath: Poliwrath is a large threat in the metagame thanks to its unique typing and immunity to Water-type moves. Poliwrath's high physical bulk allows it to wall a large portion of the metagame, including Carracosta and Sneasel. With the recent Musharna ban, Poliwrath has only increased in usefulness and usage! Poliwrath is a prime defensive Pokémon and is extremely common.
Sneasel: Thanks to Sneasel's high Speed , it is able to outspeed the vast majority of the unboosted metagame. This Speed backed with Sneasel's powerful Knock Off and high Attack is a perfect recipe for a threat. Sneasel also has access to a powerful priority move in Ice Shard and a great neutral coverage with its STABs alone. And, while its effectiveness has decreased slightly since the beginning of the tier, Sneasel is still a large threat that has to be accounted for when teambuilding. [WIP]
