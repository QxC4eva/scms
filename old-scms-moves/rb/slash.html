[SHORT DESC] High critical hit rate. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage and has a high critical hit rate.

Competitive Use

Despite its poor Base Power, its main user, Persian, has a 100% critical hit rate with it, so it's an incredible wallbreaking attack that can go through all Reflect users. No defense boost will stop this attack from 2HKOing Alakazam and 3HKOing Chansey. Other than that, it's outclassed by Double-Edge and Body Slam on slower Pokemon.

Related Moves

Body Slam is a more powerful alternative which boasts a 30% paralyze chance.

Double-Edge is a much stronger option with recoil.
