Introduction

Speed is just as important in Little Cup as it is in any other tier. In some ways, it is even more important, because a lot of Pokemon are quite frail, there are a few benchmarks you should aim to hit/pass. Below is a list of all eligible little cup Pokemon in order of their speed. With this list, you can hopefully grow your knowledge of the tier and improve your skills in Little Cup.
