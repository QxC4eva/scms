[SHORT DESC] Records battles over Wi-Fi or in the Battle Subway. Also tracks the number of Battle Points earned. [LONG DESC]

Description

Vs. Recorder is an item that will keep track of selected battles as video recordings, allows you to see your progress in the Battle Subways, and keeps a record of your Battle Points.

Location

When you first enter the Battle Subway, an NPC gives you the Vs. Recorder.
