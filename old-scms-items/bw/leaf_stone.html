[SHORT DESC] Evolves Gloom, Weepinbell, Exeggcute, Nuzleaf, and Pansage. [LONG DESC]

Description

Evolves Gloom into Vileplume, Weepinbell into Victreebel, Exeggcute into Exeggutor, Nuzleaf into Shiftry, and Pansage into Simisage.

Location

Route 6, Castelia City, Black City (Pokemon Black only), and the dust clouds.
