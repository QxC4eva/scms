[SHORT DESC] Puts the foe to sleep. [LONG DESC]

Description

Puts the target to sleep.

Competitive Use

Hypnosis has little competitive use. It's mainly used by lead Gengar to get the fastest sleep. It's very unreliable, but if you happen to put your opponent asleep, you will have a huge advantage in battle.

Related Moves

Sing is a Normal-type equivalent.

Sleep Powder is a more accurate equivalent.

Lovely Kiss is a more accurate Normal-type equivalent.

Spore is a much more accurate equivalent.
