[SHORT DESC] 30% chance to flinch the target. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage and has a 30% chance to flinch the target.

Competitive Use

While Iron Head is a well rounded move, Steel is a poor attacking type. Therefore, Iron Head is rare. The only common user of this move is Jirachi, whose Serene Grace ability doubles the move's flinch chance to 60%. Subsequently, Iron Head becomes an extremely useful move, able to flinch slower (or paralyzed) foes multiple times, which can result in KOes. Iron Head Jirachi frequently carries a means of paralysis, and is able to outspeed and threaten an absurd number of Pokemon with this strategy.

Related Moves

Meteor Mash is a more powerful alternative, but with significantly lower distribution.
