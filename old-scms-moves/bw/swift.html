[SHORT DESC] Ignores evasion modifiers. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage and ignores all accuracy and evasion modifiers.

Competitive Use

Swift has a small niche as specially based Ambipom's and Persian's only good Normal-type STAB. Both these Pokemon possess Technician, which powers Swift up to a hearty 90 Base Power before STAB, and compensates for their low base Special Attack. Beyond that use, however, Swift is best ignored for some other coverage option or Tri Attack when available.

Related Moves

Tri Attack is a more powerful option with a chance to burn, freeze, or paralyze the target.

Hyper Voice is a more powerful option.

Magical Leaf is a Grass-type equivalent.

Shock Wave is an Electric-type equivalent.

Aerial Ace is a physical Flying-type equivalent.

Faint Attack is a physical Dark-type equivalent.

Magnet Bomb is a physical Steel-type equivalent.

Shadow Punch is a physical Ghost-type equivalent.
