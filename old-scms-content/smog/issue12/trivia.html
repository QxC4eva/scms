Trivia Answers

1. What were the first Pokémon from the second, third, and fourth generation that were shown in America?
Gen 2: Ho-oh / Gen 3: Azurill, Kecleon, Wailmer / Gen 4: Munchlax. In the case of Gen III, they were all revealed at the same time.
2. Which Pokémon become slower when they evolve?
Caterpie, Weedle, Poliwhirl, Magneton, Onix, Tangela, Scyther, Electabuzz, Magmar, Murkrow, Remoraid, Wurmple, Wingull, Surskit, Anorith, Combee. Some missed a few, some got all of them.
3. Which is the most common type combination in dual-typed Pokémon? (The order of the types is irrelevant.)
Normal/Flying.
4. Name two Pokémon whose level-up movepool becomes smaller upon evolution.
Any of: Arcanine, Togekiss, Vileplume, Bellossom, Wobbuffet, Cascoon, Silcoon, Metapod, Kakuna, and Ludicolo. The pre-evolution was acceptable as well.
5. In the Water 3 Egg group, the Pokémon have a total of seven types. What are they?
Water, Ice, Poison, Bug, Rock, Grass, Psychic. A lot of people said Dark as well, due to Drapion, but I said "Water 3", meaning Water 3 only. Drapion is in two different Egg groups, and so doesn't qualify. Sorry, folks!
6. Out of the 11 moves with a 30% flinch rate, which is has the highest Base Power, and which one the lowest?
Sky Attack sits high with 140, while Astonish is down at 30.
7. In Generation IV, name all fully evolved Fighting-types with only one ability.
Blaziken, Medicham, Infernape, Gallade, Fist Arceus. A-ha!, weren't expecting Arceus, eh? Think outside the box!
8. Which Flying-type Pokémon obtainable before the Elite Four (across all generations) has the highest Special Attack?
Rayquaza. Emerald version. Makes the game easy, but hey.
9. In Pokémon Ranger: Shadows of Almia, how many Pokémon have the Field Ability "Recharge" of any value?
7. There was no need to name them, but out of interest they were: Pichu, Pikachu, Raichu, Magnemite, Magneton, Chinchou and Lanturn.
10. Of all the "Serpentine" body-style Pokémon, which three have the highest Attack, in descending order from first to third?
Rayquaza, Gyarados, Huntail.
