[SHORT DESC] Lowers target's accuracy by 1 stage. [LONG DESC]

Description

Lowers the target's accuracy by one stage. Pokemon protected by Magic Coat or the ability Magic Bounce are unaffected and instead use this move themselves. When used in-game, Flash will illuminate dark caves. The effect will last until the player exits the cave and then reset when the player enters the cave again.

Competitive Use

Flash has little to no competitive use. Using moves that affect accuracy is an ineffective strategy since many players switch their Pokemon out often, resetting the accuracy boosts.

Related Moves

Sand-Attack is a Ground-type equivalent.

Kinesis is a Psychic-type equivalent.

SmokeScreen is a Normal-type equivalent.

Mud-Slap deals damage along with lowering the opponent's accuracy one stage.
