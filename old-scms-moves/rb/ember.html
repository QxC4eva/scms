[SHORT DESC] 10% chance to burn. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage and has a 10% chance to burn the target.

Competitive Use

Do not use this move. Most, if not all, Pokemon that get Ember also have Flamethrower or Fire Blast, so use these moves instead.

Related Moves

Flamethrower is a more powerful alternative.

Fire Blast is a less accurate but more powerful alternative with a 30% chance to burn.
