Welcome to the fourth installment of A Trivial Showdown! Basically, answer as many of the questions given below as you can, and once you think you've got them, PM your solutions to The Player on Smogon. Correctly answered Easy questions are worth 1 point, Medium questions are worth 2 points, Hard questions are worth 3 points, and the Bonus question is worth 5 points. We'll announce the winners (the three people with the most points) when the next issue of The Player rolls around. Prizes will be given out to the winners. Good luck and have fun!

Puzzles

Question: Hint

Easy - Hangman. Fill out the rest of the word according to the hint.

  1. _ _ _ _ L _ _ _ E _: PS public room.
  2. _ K _ _ _ _ _ _ _: Thanked on the PS Credits page.
  3. _ _ _ _ _ E _: Leader on PS.
  4. _ _ _ O _: Former Global Moderator on PS.
  5. _ _ X _ _ /_ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _: Global Voice on PS.

Medium - Anagrams. Switch around the order of the letters to receive a PS-related answer. (ex. Need Hug Gun would be Hugendugen)

  1. Laden Nerds: A room on PS.
  2. A Cleopatra Pen: An Other Meta.
  3. Hate Catnip: A Global Staff member on PS.
  4. Focal Waterpower: A PS Global Voice and a public room they own.

Hard - "Psywaves" Questions. Use the clues to find synonyms that complete the full word. Be careful because this type of question is based on pronunciation. For example, when a clue is "A Letter," the letter may be "R" but spelled "ar" in the word. (ex. "Onomatopoeia + Hole + A Letter" would be Pupitar. Pew + Pit + Ar)

  1. Center + Award: Battle format on PS.
  2. Impair + Letter: PS Global Staff member.
  3. Pronoun + Animal: PS Global Voice.

1) Find the former PS Administrator/Leader with the fewest posts on the Smogon Forums and then find the user who posted the first VM on their profile. Then multiply the number of times that user was mentioned by the number of votes that user received in the poll about the best custom avatar on PS.
