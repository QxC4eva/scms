[SHORT DESC] 30% chance to flinch foe. [LONG DESC]

Description

Has a 30% chance to flinch the target.

Competitive Use

Low Kick's flinch rate is nice, but its Base Power is too low. It's usually better to use other better Fighting-type options, such as Submission.

Related Moves

Submission is a more powerful option that inflicts recoils on the user.
