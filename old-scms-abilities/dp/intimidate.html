[SHORT DESC] Lowers the foe's Attack one stage. Decreases wild encounter rate. [LONG DESC]

Lowers the enemy's Attack stat one stage upon entering the battlefield. Affects both opponents in doubles. Activates even if gained through Trace, Skill Swap, or Role Play. When a Pokémon with this ability is first in the party, wild encounters have a lower chance of occurring.

If a Pokémon uses Intimidate, that Pokémon cannot use it again until it leaves play, even if it loses and re-gains Intimidate through Skill Swap.
