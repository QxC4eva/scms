[SHORT DESC] Increases the power of recoil moves by 20%. [LONG DESC]

The following moves have their power increased by 20%:

  • Double-Edge
  • Flare Blitz
  • Head Smash
  • Hi Jump Kick
  • Jump Kick
  • Submission
  • Take Down
  • Volt Tackle
  • Wood Hammer

      • Struggle, although thought of as a recoil move, is not boosted by this ability. The item Life Orb has no effect on this ability.
