[SHORT DESC] Raises accuracy by 30% and increases the probability of wild Pokémon holding items. [LONG DESC]

Increases the accuracy of all moves used by this Pokémon by 30%. This is a multiplicative increase, not additive, meaning if a move has 50% accuracy before Compoundeyes, it is brought to 65% total accuracy afterward.

When a Pokémon with this ability is first in the party, wild Pokémon will have a higher chance of holding an item. This effect only applies to items that the Pokémon can be found holding regardless.
