[title] NeverUsed Resources [head] [page]

If you are new to NeverUsed, then this page will provide various resources that may be helpful to you. If you have any further questions in regards to the tier, then post in the NU Simple Questions, Simple Answers thread and people will be more than happy to answer you.

Resources

ORAS NU Sample Teams: This thread is mostly for new users trying to learn the basics of NU teambuilding. Check it out if you're at a loss or want to pick up some expert advice from veteran players!

NU Information: This thread contains information about how NU is run.

Tiering Information: This thread is available for everyone to see the reasoning behind each suspect test's decision.

Good Cores in ORAS NU: This thread is for discussion pairs of Pokemon and how they complement each other to form a solid core.

Guides

ORAS NU Speed Tiers: This resource contains the Speed stat of every Pokemon in NU and allows for players to quickly see how many Speed EVs they need to run in order to Speed creep, among other things.

Helpful Articles from The Smog

Creative Movesets in XY NU: This article explains some sets that are uncommon or underrated on a Pokemon, and discusses why it should be used more.

Weather in NU: This article explains some of the common Pokemon used on weather teams, for either setting or taking advantage of the weather.

"Now Playing" Threads

"Now Playing" or np: threads are are the centerpiece of every round of the NU metagame. General discussion of each metagame will usually be found in the np: thread, and a new one is posted after every NU suspect test or significant changes in the metagame.

  • np: NU Stage 4 - Celebration

  • np: NU Stage 1 - We Might Be Dead By Tomorrow
  • np: NU Stage 0 - Welcome to the NU Age

NeverUsed Contributions & Corrections

All the analyses and guides featured on Smogon don't just write themselves! Everything that is written must go through a process that involves quality and grammar control before going on-site. If you are interested in contributing, or you just want to see the up-to-date sets currently being worked on, check out the NU Analyses subforum. Be sure to go look at through all the important threads to get a good idea of everything if you are want to contribute.
