[SHORT DESC] A Ball used to catch Pokemon that becomes more effective as turns pass. [LONG DESC]

Description

A Ball used to catch Pokemon. Will fail during Trainer battles. Cannot be used in Link or Wi-Fi battles.

Timer Ball has a capture modifier of *1 if less than 10 turns have passed, *2 if 10 to 19 turns have passed, *3 if 20 to 29 turns have passed, and *4 if more than 29 turns have passed. The capture modifier does not go higher than *4.
