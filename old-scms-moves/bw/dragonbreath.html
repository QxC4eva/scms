[SHORT DESC] 30% chance to paralyze opponent. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage and has a 30% chance to paralyze the target.

Competitive Use

DragonBreath is an inferior alternative to Dragon Pulse and Draco Meteor on any specially-oriented sets. The paralysis-inducing effect does not make up for its low Base Power.

Related Moves

Draco Meteor has much higher Base Power, but lowers the user's Special Attack by two stages.

Spacial Rend is much stronger but has terrible distribution.

Dragon Pulse has no secondary effects but higher Base Power.

Discharge is a stronger Electric-type variant.
