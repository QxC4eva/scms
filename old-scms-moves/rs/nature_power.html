[SHORT DESC] Attack changes based on terrain. [LONG DESC]

Nature Power turns into another attack based on terrain.

  • Wi-Fi: Tri Attack
  • Gateway: Hydro Pump
  • Main Street: Tri Attack
  • Waterfall: Seed Bomb
  • Neon: Tri Attack
  • Crystal: Rock Slide
  • Sunny Park: Seed Bomb
  • Magma: Rock Slide
  • Sunset: Earthquake
  • Courtyard: Tri Attack
  • Stargazer: Rock Slide
