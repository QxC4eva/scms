[SHORT DESC] Prevents Steel-type Pokémon from switching. Increases the chance of encountering a Steel-type Pokémon in the wild. [LONG DESC]

If the enemy is a Steel-type, they cannot switch out. Baton Pass functions normally. When a Pokémon with this ability is first in the party, wild encounters with Steel-type Pokémon have a higher chance of occurring. (Emerald only.)
