[SHORT DESC] Boosts user's Defense one stage. [LONG DESC]

Description

Boosts the user's Defense by one stage.

Competitive Use

Harden is an utterly useless move and should never be used seriously. Its only value is the comedic one when used by Metapod, and that got old about twelve years ago. Don't use this move.

Related Moves

Acid Armor has less PP, but raises Defense by two stages.

Barrier has less PP, but raises Defense by two stages.

Reflect doubles the Defense stat up to 1024 Defense instead of 999.
