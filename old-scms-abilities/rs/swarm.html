[SHORT DESC] Boosts power of Bug-type moves 50% when at 1/3 HP or less. [LONG DESC]

When Pokémon with this ability are at 1/3 HP or less, all of their Bug-type moves will have 50% more power.

When a Pokémon with this ability is first in the party, wild Pokémon cries in the area are heard more often while walking.

Related Abilities

Blaze is a Fire-type equivalent.
Overgrow is a Grass-type equivalent.
Torrent is a Water-type equivalent.
