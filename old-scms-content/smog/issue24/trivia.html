Trivia

Hello, folks! Don't mind this head bandage, or this arm cast, or this IV needle. I'm completely fine from my *ahem* fall down the stairs. That's why I was gone last issue. American health care is extremely inferior to the NHS. I asked for some ibuprofen and the nurse brought me nachos. I think that sums up my experience quite well.

In any case, I appreciate that Level 51 filled in for me last issue. Did he do a good job? Do leave feedback—maybe it'll give me the chance to go on vacation more often!

So, without further ado, here's the answers to his questions.

  1. How many abilities are only available as Dream World abilities?
    15—Imposter, Toxic Boost, Zen Mode, Flare Boost, Multiscale, Harvest, Magic Bounce, Contrary, Friend Guard, Heavy Metal, Light Metal, Pickpocket, Moody, Rattled, and Analytic.
  2. Name all the HGSS TMs that are available through the use of the Pokéwalker.
    TM07 Hail from Icy Mountain Rd., TM10 Hidden Power and TM15 Hyper Beam from Quiet Cave, TM11 Sunny Day from Blue Lake, TM18 Rain Dance from Dim Cave, TM29 Psychic from Night Sky's Edge, TM37 Sandstorm from Town Outskirts, and TM 68 Giga Impact from White Lake.
  3. Which Pokemon takes the least damage from Choice Band Rampardos's Head Smash, assuming they hold no items?
    In terms of absolute damage, it's Shedinja, which takes only 1 damage. If we're talking about percentages, it's technically a Blissey that has Mimicked Transform and Transformed into Steelix.
  4. What is the most common BST among all LC Pokémon? How many Pokémon share this BST?
    The most common BST in Little Cup is 300, and 18 Pokémon (Bagon, Baltoy, Beldum, Bellsprout, Bronzor, Croagunk, Deino, Dratini, Geodude, Gible, Klink, Larvitar, Minccino, Poliwag, Remoraid, Sandshrew, Snorunt, and Snubbull), share a BST of 300.
  5. Name all the Pokemon that can appear in multiple levels of the Battle Pyramid.
    Raichu, Jolteon, Haunter, Gengar, Ludicolo, Ninetales, Dusclops, Banette, Misdreavus, Shedinja, Absol, Weezing, Golem, Pineco, Forretress, Shiftry, Wobbuffet, Slowking, Starmie, Steelix, Shuckle, Armaldo, Heracross, Quagsire, Lapras, Cacturne, Flareon, Walrein, Gyarados, Scizor, Sneasel, Houndoom, Dewgong, Sableye, Aerodactyl, and Poliwrath.
  6. Black Tower is how many floors tall?
    20. Note that this figure also includes the Ground Floor, i.e. the lobby!
  7. My King's Rock Jirachi has just used Iron Head on my foe's Umbreon. What is the chance of it flinching?
    The chance is 60% if Umbreon has Synchronize (King's Rock does not affect Iron Head in Gen V), or 0% if it has Inner Focus.
  8. The music in the Castelia Sewers can be found where else?
    Virbank Complex
  9. Who is the only person to have won two Pokémon TCG World Championships? In what years did (s)he win them?
    Jason Klaczynski won the Pokémon TCG World Championships in 2006 and 2008.
  10. Name all the legendary trios ever to not have equal BSTs among all three members.
    Articuno/Zapdos/Moltres (in Gen I!), Tornadus/Thundurus/Landorus, Groudon/Kyogre/Rayquaza, Reshiram/Zekrom/Kyurem.
