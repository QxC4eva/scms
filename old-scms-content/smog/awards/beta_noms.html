This is the nomination form for the 2014 Smog Awards! Please do not submit more than once as only the first submission will be counted. You should only nominate 1 user per category. Don't nominate yourself, either! Additionally, to nominate a user, you should type out their full, current username. Abbreviated or out-of-date names may not be counted.
