[SHORT DESC] User is made invulnerable for one turn, then hits the next turn. 30% chance to paralyze. [LONG DESC]

User is made invulnerable for one turn, then hits the next turn. Has a 30% chance to paralyze the target. Sky Uppercut and Thunder have regular accuracy and Base Power against a Pokémon in the middle of Bounce. The Base Power of Gust and Twister doubles against a Pokémon in the charge up stage of Bounce. If the user takes damage from sandstorm and hail normally, it will continue to do so on the charge turn.

Related moves

Fly is a stronger, more accurate equivalent, but lacks a chance of paralysis.
