[SHORT DESC] Removes Leech Seed, Spikes, Stealth Rock, and Toxic Spikes. [LONG DESC]

Removes Leech Seed from the user. Clears the user's side of Spikes, Stealth Rock, and Toxic Spikes. Frees the user from Bind, Clamp, Fire Spin, Magma Storm, Sand Tomb, Whirlpool, and Wrap. Has no effect if used on a Ghost. Has no effect if it misses.
