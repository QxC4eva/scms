[SHORT DESC] A Ball used to catch Pokemon that is more effective at night. [LONG DESC]

Description

This Ball is useful, as it has an enormous catch rate of 3.5 when in a dark area (night-time or a cave). It's catch rate is otherwise 1.

Competitive Use

This item has no competitive use.

Location

Striaton City, Castelia City. It can also be bought on Route 9, or in Driftveil City, Opelucid City, or the Pokemon League.
