Description

Locks the user into this move for 2-3 turns. Confuses the user at the end.

Competitive Use

While Petal Dance may seem like a competitively viable move due to its power, it is usually not recommended to use due to its poor coverage. Grass-type attacks are resisted by a fairly large amount of common threats, and you probably don't want to be locked into a move like this.

Related Moves

Razor Leaf is a weaker alternative that does not lock the user into the move and has a high critical rate.
