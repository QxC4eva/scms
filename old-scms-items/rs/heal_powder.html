[SHORT DESC] Removes all status effects from one Pokemon, but has a bitter taste. [LONG DESC]

Description

Removes all status problems, as well as confusion, from one Pokemon, but reduces happiness by 5 points if the targets happiness level is between 0-199, and reduces the targets happiness level by 10 points if its happiness level is between 200-249. One-time use. Cannot be used in Link or Wi-Fi battles.
