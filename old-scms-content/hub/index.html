Community Hubs

The Community Hubs feature various information about key aspects of the Smogon community, how to get involved, and other useful resources.

  • Rate My Team showcases great past teams and gives players a chance to get critiques on their own teams.
  • Wi-Fi features RNG guides, trade threads, and our Wi-Fi Battle Finder.
  • Orange Islands is for everything related to casual Pokemon, and home to The Orange League and Movie Nights.
  • Smogon IRC is home for rules and information about #pokemon on SynIRC, Smogon's official chat, as well as the other semi-official channels.
