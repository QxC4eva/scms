[SHORT DESC] Grants access to the S.S. Aqua and S.S. Anne to take the player to various locations. [LONG DESC]

Description

Grants access to the S.S. Aqua to take the player to Lilycove and Slateport City. It also lets the player visit the Battle Tower in Ruby and Sapphire, and the Battle Frontier in Emerald.

Grants access to the S.S. Anne in Vermillion City in FireRed and LeafGreen.
