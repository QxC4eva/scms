[SHORT DESC] A Ball used to catch Pokemon that is more effective against lower level Pokemon. [LONG DESC]

Description

A Ball used to catch Pokemon. Nest Balls cannot be used during Trainer Battles. Will fail during Trainer battles. Cannot be used in Link or Wi-Fi battles.

Nest Ball has a capture rate modifier of *(40 - level) / 10. If the target's level is between 1 and 10, the modifier is *3. If the target's level is between 11 and 20, the modifier is *2. If the target's level is between 21 and 100, the modifier is *1.
