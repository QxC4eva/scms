Shoddy Battle was the most popular Pokémon simulator of the Diamond/Pearl generation. Smogon hosted a Shoddy Battle server as a service to the competitive community. Since then, it has been replaced by Pokémon Laboratory.

To download Shoddy Battle, visit this page.

For Shoddy Battle support or other inquiries related to the program, please post in the Shoddy Battle forum.
