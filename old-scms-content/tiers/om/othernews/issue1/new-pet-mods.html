  • Gen Wun — by: Jajoken
    • A complete revamp of the game which reverts all Pokémon to Generation One mechanics and stats. All Pokémon receive a custom revamp.

  • Project DPP — by: Monte Cristo
    • This mod attempts to take Pokémon and reform them to their former glory days by changing multiple Pokémon to conform to the DPP era.

  • Joimmons — by: Joim
    • Joimmons is a custom modification made by Joim to create a balanced metagame, and, well, because he liked it!

  • Hidden OU — by: Eevee General
    • Hidden OU is a batch of super secretive, hidden leaks that are leaked to Eevee General that contain many Pokémon ability changes!

  • Nonsense Theorymons — by: DialgaTheTimeLord
    • Based off of OU Theorymon, DialgaTheTimeLord has created a community Mod where users suggest nonsense theorymons, as the title implies!
