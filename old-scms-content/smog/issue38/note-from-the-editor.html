Hi!

We hope you enjoyed this edition of The Smog.

A lot of people seemed to notice the lack of an Editor's Note in the last issue. It might have seemed like I just forgot, but it was actually purposeful. The reason is simple: I didn't really have anything different or important to say, so why force it?

You should also notice that our releases are getting more frequent. We're hoping that this change helps us to deliver content that is more relevant and serves to give everyone a more predictable time frame for each issue. This has the natural side effect of smaller issues, but we don't believe this is a bad thing; it also led to a new way of categorizing our content that we hope is more accurate and puts our most popular content front and center.

As always, we'd like to thank our readers and contributors.

I think that's it for now. If you have questions or comments on the issue or any of these changes, feel free to head on over to the release thread, send an email to thesmog[at]smogon[dot]com, or message any of the staff directly.

Thanks again!

- Toast++
