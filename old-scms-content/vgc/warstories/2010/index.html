Introduction

The 2010 VGC marked a major change to the ruleset, suddenly allowing up to two of the titular lengendaries, such as Kyogre and Giratina. This obviously hugely influences the play, and changed the strategy from years prior. VGC 2010 made no real increase to the number of events, but US events dropped the 128 limit lottery in favor of a 768 first-come, first-served limit, which stopped many people from being turned away without playing. VGC 2010 did add two new events, a Last Chance Qualifier at both the National Championships and World Championships, but made the National Championships qualification only. An auto-level feature was also included in the new HeartGold and SoulSilver games and so the de facto ban on Tyranitar and most Dragonite was lifted, while also giving some Pokémon access to moves they wouldn't have otherwise had.
