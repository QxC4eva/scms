[SHORT DESC] Removes the charge up turn from all moves. One-time use. [LONG DESC]

Description

Removes the charge up turn from Bounce, Dig, Dive, Fly, Razor Wind, Shadow Force, Skull Bash, Sky Attack, and SolarBeam. Skull Bash still receives the Defense boost. Giga Impact, Hyper Beam, Roar of Time, and Rock Wrecker still need to recharge after use. One-time use.
