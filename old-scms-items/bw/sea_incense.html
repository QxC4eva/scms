[SHORT DESC] Raises power of Water-type moves 20%. Allows breeding of Azurill. [LONG DESC]

Description

Increases the power of all of the holder's damage-dealing Water-type attacks by 20%. If Marill or Azumarill breeds while holding Sea Incense, an Azurill Egg will be produced as opposed to a Marill Egg.

Competitive Use

Sea Incense is practically identical to Mystic Water, as they both boost Water-type moves by 20%. In any case, Sea Incense is not used much in competitive battles. It is often outclassed by items such as Choice Band and Choice Specs, which boost the power of physical and special moves by 1.5, respectively, and Life Orb, which boosts the power of all moves by 1.3. Although these three items do come with negative effects, it is generally better to use them over a Sea Incense.

Location

Driftveil Market (Post-National Pokédex) - 9,600 Poké Dollars.
