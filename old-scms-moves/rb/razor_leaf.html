Description

Has a high critical hit rate.

Competitive Use

Razor Leaf is the best Grass-type move with no drawbacks. It has a high critical rate, meaning Venusaur will get a lot of critical hits using this attack, and it will bypass negative and positive boosts, being a very good move to deal with Amnesia Slowbro.

Related Moves

SolarBeam is a two-turn stronger alternative.

Mega Drain is a weaker alternative that heals the user half the damage done.
