  • Setupmons — by: Ghoul King
    • All Pokemon gain access to every stat boosting move that boost a maximum of +2. For example, Swords Dance boosts Attack +2, so it's included. Dragon Dance boosts Attack +1 and Speed +1, which is a max of +2, so it's included. See link for full list.
    • Playable as a custom game on any server

  • Move Equality — by: Yuelia
    • All moves that deal non-priority damage now have 90 power and 100 accuracy, while their side effects remain identical.

  • Last Bastion — by: TemporaryAnonymous
    • Doubles format. When an allied Pokemon faints, the surviving ally's Attack, Defense, Special Attack, and Special Defense are boosted +1 each. Self-KO moves and friendly fire do not activate the boosts.

  • Little Legends Cup — by: Heroes and Cons
    • LC format. All Legendaries have their base stats halved, rounded up, and then increased by either 5, 10, 15, 20, or 25 depending on tier.
    • Playable on the Aqua Server

  • Turnmons — by: Xmas121
    • Only non-damaging attacks are usable on turns 1-5, then only damaging attacks are usable turns 6-10. This pattern repeats every 5 turns.
    • Playable on the Aqua Server

  • Gods and Followers — by: Jajoken
    • Pick 1 Pokemon from any tier (including Ubers) and designate it your 'god.' The rest of your team must share at least one type with that Pokemon, called 'followers.' Once your 'god' faints, your team suffers -1 priority to its moves.
    • Playable on the Aqua Server

  • Volturn Mayhem — by: Rumors
    • All attacks that target the opponent now cause the user to switch out. For example, Fake Out, Will-O-Wisp, and Roar all target the opponent and cause your Pokemon to switch after use.
    • Playable on the Aqua Server

  • Nature's Blessing — by: Djqubi
    • In this metagame, if a Pokemon is holding a type-resisting berry, it will receive an immunity to this type.
    • Playable on the Aqua Server
