Archived Projects

UU Team Study is a project that attempts to unite the community while improving the overall quality of the UU playerbase by studying the art of teambuilding at the highest level.

Counter This Threat focuses on a major threat in the UU metagame and asks users to describe sets that can beat it.

NU and RU Pokemon in UU is a thread that takes a look at Pokemon from the lower tiers and determines their worth in UU. This is a great resource for players who are looking to incorporate some creativity in their teams, but are unsure of which RU and NU pokemon are viable in UU.

The Taking a look at BL thread is for discussing the impact BL Pokemon would have in the current UU tier. This thread is strictly for theorymon, it is in no way a step towards re-testing BL Pokemon in UU.

The Next Best Thing... thread is an ongoing project that encourages players from all experience levels to create new sets for already on-site Pokemon. This link will take you to the sets that were considered to be the best entries per round, and maybe give you some unique team ideas.

Darwinian Theory

Otherwise known as Survival of the Fittest, is a project that combines community discussion with community battles. Two teams are posted and users argue over the winner while voting on which users will use the teams in battle.
