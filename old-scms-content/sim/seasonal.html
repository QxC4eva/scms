[title] Pokemon Showdown Seasonal Ladder [head] [page]
By Joim.

Seasonal Ladder is a tier hosted on Pokemon Showdown. Much like Random Battle, the Seasonal Ladder lets you play with randomly generated teams; however, not every Pokemon can be randomly generated. The Pokemon that may be generated are based on themes relating to the time of the year. The current theme is Winter's Wont.

This month's theme is winter and its capricious ways. The Pokémon that may be found on this Seasonal are mainly ice-types and winter themed Pokémon. There's permanent hail due to winter's inclemency. Additionally, Pokémon are fifty times levels higher and the typechart used is that of Inverse metagame The game type is Doubles.
