[SHORT DESC] 10% chance to boosts user's Attack by 1 stage. [LONG DESC]

Description

Deals damage and has a 10% chance to boost the user's Attack by one stage.

Competitive Use

Metal Claw should not be used competitively. It is outclassed by stronger moves, such as Iron Head. Furthermore, Steel is a poor attacking type.

Related Moves

Iron Head is a more powerful alternative that has a chance to flinch the target.

Meteor Mash is a far more powerful equivalent, with smaller distribution.
