[SHORT DESC] Summons permanent sandstorm. Decreases wild encounter rate when in a sandstorm. [LONG DESC]

When this Pokémon is brought out, a permanent sandstorm covers the battlefield. It can only be removed by bringing out a Pokémon with Drizzle, Drought, or Snow Warning, or by using Rain Dance, Sunny Day, or Hail.

Smogon has an article on this topic.

When a Pokémon with this ability is first in the party, wild encounters have a lower chance of occurring, but only when venturing through an area affected by sandstorm.

Related Abilities

Drizzle is a rain equivalent.
Drought is a sun equivalent.
Snow Warning is a hail equivalent.
