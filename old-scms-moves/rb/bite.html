Description

Has a 10% chance to flinch.

Competitive Use

Bite is too weak to be considered competitively. Double-Edge and Body Slam are much better Normal-type attacking options.

Related Moves

Slash is a more powerful move with a high critical hit rate.

Double-Edge is a more powerful alternative with no secondary effects but inflicts recoil on the user.

Body Slam is a more powerful move with a 30% chance to paralyze opponents.
