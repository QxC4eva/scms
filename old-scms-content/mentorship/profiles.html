About

Below is a list of every Smogon Mentor. You'll find information on where they participate in Smogon, when they're available, and what their interests are. You can message any of them on the forums if you have questions about Smogon. They are all friendly people who are outstanding members in our community, and each one of them would love to talk with you. Don't be afraid to message them; they want to hear from you!
