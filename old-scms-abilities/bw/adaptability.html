[SHORT DESC] The wielder's STAB modifier becomes 2x. [LONG DESC]

Description

This ability alters the wielder's STAB to be 2x instead of 1.5x.

Competitive Use

This ability is very useful on paper, but it has a very low distribution, with many of the possible users rarely being used in most tiers.
