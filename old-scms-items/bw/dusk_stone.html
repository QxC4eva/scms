[SHORT DESC] Evolves Misdreavus, Murkrow, and Lampent. [LONG DESC]

Description

An evolution stone used to evolve Misdreavus into Mismagius, Murkrow into Honchkrow, and Lampent into Chandelure.

Competitive Use

This item is only needed to obtain the aforementioned Pokemon, and otherwise has no competitive use.

Location

A Dusk Stone can be found on Route 10 and Mistralton Cave, or in a dustcloud in any of the following locations: Wellspring Cave, Chargestone Cave, Mistralton Cave, Twist Mountain, Challengers Cave, Victory Road, and Giant Chasm.
