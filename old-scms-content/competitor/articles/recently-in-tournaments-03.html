Smogon Grand Slam 3

The Smogon Grand Slam is a one-of-a-kind tournament where players compete in six separate opens to earn points. Each open hosts a different tier, namely Ubers, UU, RU, NU, LC and Doubles. With the introduction of Doubles to the tournament, some changes were made to the ruleset. The players can only play 5 out of the 6 opens. The eight combatants that earn the most points advance to the final bracket, where they compete in best of fives until one player remains to claim the prestigious cyan trophy . You can keep track of the top eight by visiting this spreadsheet.

All the opens are still going on, and the participants are still trying as hard as they can for the top 8 spots. You can keep track of the happenings of the different opens through their forum threads, which can all be found here.

World Cup of Pokémon 9

VS.

The World Cup of Pokémon, one of Smogon's two official team tournaments, is one of the most prestigious events in online Pokémon. Sixteen regions from all over the world compete in the first round, where the top eight teams are decided. After being placed in a bracket, the remaining eight teams go head to head until one comes out on top. The winning team, starters and substitutes alike, are awarded the iconic dark blue trophy .

Last year US East showed a dominating performance, beating Asia in the finals 5-2. Considered the heavy favorites this year, East made nothing but improvements to their winning roster, adding Sinclair, PDC, and aim, all considered excellent players in XY OU. Showing no signs of slipping up, East finished group stages as the number one seed with an impressive 18-6 record. Three games behind East with a solid 15-9, we found US West, who improved last year's roster by adding reyscarface, Dice, Ben Gay, fingerscrossed and Valentine. At 14-10 we had three capable teams, namely US Metro, Germany, and Spain. The remaining three teams, Brazil, Asia, and Canada, all managed to get by groups with 13-11 records.

After seeding was determined, these eight teams were placed in the Quarterfinal bracket. East, as expected, was the team with the most impressive performance, beating out Canada 6-2. The other Quarterfinal victors, West, Brazil, and Spain, were victorious over Asia, Metro, and Germany respectively, with 5-3 records. In the next round, East crushed their Semifinal opponents, Spain, 7-1 and started to prepare for the finals. In a back and forth series, Brazil was able pull off the upset, eliminating the second seed, US West, 5-3.

Hill lost, BlueStar won the second, Destiny Device won another, Dekzeh lost, HANTSUKI won, Kratos won, Malfoy lost, Sogeking won the last.

Smogon Frontier 8

The new season of Smogon Frontier is underway. This time there have been some changes to the format, in order to make it a little tougher for the challengers to conquer the frontier, as well as removing the problems of brain fatigue. From now on, there are going to be brains for the XY OU tier, as well as, BW2 OU, DPP OU and ADV OU. Also, there will be 16 brains, of which, the challengers will have to beat 15. For more information, check out the official thread here. You can check out the current standings here. As of now, user Bluwing came closest to winning it with 10 wins, but he was defeated by Palace Maven Ojama, Arcade Star Dice, and Factory Foreman crashinboombang, ending his run. Sand Castle and Dekzeh got 9 wins, followed by Ojama with 8 wins before getting disqualified.

Smogon Tour 18

The new season of Smogon Tour is starting on September 5th. It is a live tournament, where people have to battle continuously until they come out on top. Players are given points on the basis of their performance. 3 tours are held each week for a period of 9 weeks, after which the 16 players with most points qualify for the playoffs and get a chance to earn this trophy . For more information, check out the Smogon Tour website and the official announcement thread.
