[SHORT DESC] Summons permanent rain. [LONG DESC]

When this Pokémon is brought out, a permanent rain covers the battlefield. It can only be removed by bringing out a Pokémon with Drought or Sand Stream, or by using Sunny Day, Sandstorm, or Hail.

If two Pokemon with weather-changing abilities are sent out at the same time, the weather of the Pokemon with the lower Speed stat will prevail.

Related Abilities

Drought is a Sun equivalent.
Sand Stream is a Sandstorm equivalent.
