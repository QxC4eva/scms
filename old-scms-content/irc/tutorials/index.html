[title] IRC Tutorials [page]

This section houses a number of beginner-friendly tutorials designed to get you connected to and chatting away on Internet Relay Chat. There is a diverse range of clients able to connect to IRC—some popular ones are listed on the sidebar, as well as a guide on connecting to IRC on your mobile device, so check them out and come join us!

If you need assistance with getting on IRC or using your client, feel free to ask for help in the Ask a Simple Question, Get a Simple Answer thread in the IRC forum.
