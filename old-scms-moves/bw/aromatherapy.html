[SHORT DESC] Cures status ailments on the user and other party members. [LONG DESC]

Description

Every Pokemon on the user's side and party is cured of its major status condition.

Competitive Use

Aromatherapy is an excellent move for use on more defensive teams as protection against status, allowing the remainder of the team to freely use Rest without necessarily having to spend 2 turns waking up. This move should ideally be used on a supporting Pokemon, for example Chansey, Blissey, or Celebi.

Related Moves

Heal Bell is a Normal-type equivalent.
