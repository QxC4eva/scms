[SHORT DESC] Doubles winnings from battle if the holder takes part. Allows breeding of Happiny. [LONG DESC]

Description

Doubles the amount of income gained at the end of in-game battles if the Pokemon that held Luck Incense took part in the battle. If Chansey or Blissey holds this item while breeding, it is possible to obtain Happiny from an Egg.

Location

Driftveil City.
