Parameter Puzzles

Your challenge for these questions is to obtain the given list of Pokemon from the /dexsearch command on Pokemon Showdown! using the correct number of parameters as shown! Note that "all" does not count as a parameter. Can you figure out which parameters were used to obtain these results?

Use the command "/dexsearch" for a quick introduction on how the command works. The first three users to send in all 15 correct answers will earn themselves a custom title on the Smogon Forums; failing which, the first three users with the highest scores will earn these custom titles instead!

  1. Level 1: Simple – the only parameters used here will be moves!
    1. Gligar, Gliscor, Pangoro, Smeargle (2 parameters)
    2. Bellossom, Gloom, Lilligant, Maractus, Smeargle, Sunflora, Vileplume (2 parameters)
    3. Masquerain, Qwilfish, Smeargle, Surskit (2 parameters)
    4. Delibird, Mew, Smeargle, Ursaring (3 parameters)
    5. Bunnelby, Diggersby, Gogoat, Mew, Skiddo, Smeargle (3 parameters)
  2. Level 2: Moderate – parameters used here may include moves, abilities, and types.
    1. Croagunk, Paras, Parasect, Toxicroak (2 parameters)
    2. Deerling, Leafeon, Sawsbuck (2 parameters)
    3. Granbull, Herdier, Snubbull, Stoutland (3 parameters)
    4. Lunatone, Meloetta, Mew, Solrock, Swoobat, Woobat (3 parameters)
    5. Avalugg, Froslass, Glalie, Snorunt (3 parameters)
  3. Level 3: Insane – parameters used here may include moves, abilities, types, colors, and exclusions!
    1. Audino, Mew, Miltank, Slowbro, Slowking, Slowpoke (3 parameters)
    2. Absol, Castform, Lugia, Smeargle (3 parameters)
    3. Finneon, Kabutops, Lumineon, Smeargle, Xerneas (3 parameters, including 1 exclusion)
    4. Bastiodon, Octillery, Remoraid, Rhydon, Rhyhorn, Rhyperior, Shieldon, Smeargle (3 parameters, including 1 exclusion)
    5. Blissey, Chansey, Flaaffy, Lickilicky, Lickitung, Mew, Miltank, Whismur (4 parameters, including 1 exclusion)

Mystery Sound

Do you consider yourself a well-traveled denizen of the Pokémon world, able to recognize a tune from just a few bars of sound? Well, this is the challenge for you! Simply guess which of the main Pokémon games and from which location in the game this clip comes from. The first two users to send in all three correct answers will win themselves a custom title on the Smogon Forums!

Crossword

And here we have it: the grand finale to this issue's Puzzle Page! To participate, simply fill out the crossword grid below with what you believe are the right answers, and send a screenshot of the grid to us. The first person to send in a completely correct grid will win themselves a custom avatar on Pokémon Showdown!, just like last issue. Should you be the lucky winner, more details concerning the prize will be sent to you later. Unlike last issue, this issue's clues could range from Pokémon names to items to abilities to moves, so keep that in mind when you examine the clues!

Participation

You are eligible for the Puzzle Page competitions as long as you have a Smogon account. To participate, send all your Puzzle Page entries in a single forum conversation to The Smog (quick link). The deadline for submission is 2 weeks from now, on the 29th of November; after this, you can still try the puzzles, but the answers will be posted here and we'll close submissions. As always, good luck and have fun!
