[SHORT DESC] Lowers physical damage done to user. [LONG DESC]

Description

Halves physical damage done to user. Does not wear away until the user switches or faints. If Reflect causes the user's Defense to reach 1024 or higher at any time during battle calculations, it will be reduced mod 1024.

Competitive Use

A very good move that halves physical damage and stops Tauros. This move makes Chansey and Alakazam able to face Tauros, Rhydon, and Golem; healing back the damage dealt in the mean time with Softboiled or Recover. Reflect Alakazam is a Pokemon to be considered for any team, as it can wall all attacks, Recover health back, and still deal powerful damage with Psychic.

Related Moves

Light Screen is a similar move that halves special damage instead.
