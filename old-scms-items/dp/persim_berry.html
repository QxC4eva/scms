[SHORT DESC] Cures confusion. One-time use. [LONG DESC]

Description

Cures confusion. Activates when the Pokemon is confused. One-time use. Provides a one-time 60 Base Power Ground-type attack when a Pokemon holding this item uses Natural Gift.

Competitive Use

There are very few reasons to use the Persim Berry. Dragon-types can use the Persim Berry to stop the confusion caused by Outrage (so they can attack again without the risk of attacking themselves). Lum Berry is usually a better option for this though, as it will also prevent other statuses from crippling your Pokemon.
